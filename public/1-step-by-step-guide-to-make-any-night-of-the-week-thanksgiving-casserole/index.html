The ingredients needed to make Thanksgiving Casserole:
  1. Make ready 2 packages instant potatoes
  2. Prepare 1 pound ground turkey
  3. Get 1/2 bag frozen mixed vegetables
  4. Get 1 can cream of chicken
  5. Take Chopped onion and garlic
  6. Make ready Seasonings (pepper, onion powder, garlic powder)
  7. Make ready 1 box stuffing
  8. Prepare 1 packet turkey gravy

Steps to make Thanksgiving Casserole:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°
  2. Brown turkey with onion, garlic, and seasonings. Drain fat. Add frozen vegetables and cream of chicken. Heat till vegetables are warm. Set a side.
  3. Make mashed potatoes per package instructions. Set a side.
  4. Make stuffing per package instructions. Set a side.
  5. In a large casserole dish layer ingredients. Mashed potatoes first, then turkey mixture, then top with stuffing. Cover with tin foil and bake for 30-40 mins until hot. Remove tin foil and cook for 10 mins for a crunchy top.
  6. Make turkey gravy per package instructions and serve with casserole.
