The ingredients needed to make Chicken Ghee Roast |Spicy Chicken Roast:
  1. Take 300 g Chicken
  2. Prepare 1 tsp Fennel seeds
  3. Get 1 tsp Red chilli powder
  4. Make ready 1 tsp Coriander powder
  5. Take 1/2 tsp Turmeric powder
  6. Take 1 chopped Onion
  7. Make ready 2 Green chillies
  8. Get 1 tbsp Ginger garlic paste
  9. Make ready 1 spring Curry leaves
  10. Get leaves Coriander
  11. Make ready 2-3 tbsp oil
  12. Take 1-2 tsp Ghee
  13. Prepare to taste Salt
  14. Take To dry roast and grind
  15. Take 2 Cloves
  16. Prepare 1/2 inch Cinnamon
  17. Get 2 Cardamom
  18. Take 1 tsp Peppercorns
  19. Make ready 1/2 tsp Cumin seeds
Steps to make Chicken Ghee Roast |Spicy Chicken Roast:
  1. Wash, clean and cut the chicken into pieces and keep aside.
  2. Heat a pan and dry roast the ingredients mentioned above for 2-3 minutes on low flame. Take them into a plate and allow it to cool.
  3. In a mixie jar/blender add the roasted ingredients and grind into a fine powder. keep aside.
  4. Heat a pan, add 2-3 tbsp oil. Once the oil is hot add fennel seeds and allow it crackle.
  5. Next, add chopped onions, slit green chillies, 1/2tsp salt and fry until onion turns into translucent.
  6. Add ginger-garlic paste and fry for 2-3 minutes until raw smell goes.
  7. Then add chicken and saut√© for 2-3 minutes on high flame.Add turmeric,chilli powder,coriander powder.Fry on a medium flame for 3-5 minutes.
  8. Add salt, curry leaves and mix well.
  9. Cover and cook for 12-15 minutes on low flame.keep stirring in the middle to avoid burning at the bottom.
  10. Open the lid and check if the chicken is cooked or not. Once the chicken is cooked add the ground masala powder and mix well.
  11. Then add 2-3 tsp of ghee and fry for 1-2 minutes on medium flame. Add coriander leaves and switch off the flame.
  12. Transfer the chicken ghee roast into a serving bowl and enjoy as a side with steamed rice, rasam or a starter.
