The ingredients needed to make Spicy Meat Sauce:
  1. Get 1 tablespoon Vegetable Oil
  2. Take 1 small piece Ginger *finely chopped OR grated
  3. Make ready 1 clove Garlic *finely chopped
  4. Get 150 g Mince Meat *Pork, Beef, Veal, Chicken, etc
  5. Take 2 Spring Onions *finely chopped
  6. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon Potato Starch OR Corn Starch *mixed with 1 teaspoon Water
  7. Make ready Sauce
  8. Make ready 1/2 tablespoon Dark Colour Miso
  9. Get 1/2 tablespoon Sugar
  10. Take 1/2 tablespoon Soy Sauce
  11. Take 1 tablespoon Sake (Rice wine)
  12. Prepare 1/2-1 tablespoon Toban Djan (Chilli Bean Sauce)
  13. Get 1/4 cup Chicken Stock *OR 1/4 cup Water & 1/4 teaspoon Asian Chicken Bouillon Powder
  14. Prepare 1 teaspoon Sesame Oil *You can add some Chilli Oil as well

Steps to make Spicy Meat Sauce:
  1. Combine all the sauce ingredients in a small bowl.
  2. Heat Oil in a saucepan over medium heat, add Ginger and Garlic. When aromatic, add Mince Meat and Spring Onion.
  3. When Minced Meat is cooked, add the sauce. Cook for a few minutes, then add Potato Starch (OR Corn Starch) mixture and stir until thickens.
