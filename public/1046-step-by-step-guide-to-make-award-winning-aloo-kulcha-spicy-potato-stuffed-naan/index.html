The ingredients needed to make üçõAloo Kulchaüçõ

(spicy potato stuffed naan):

  1. Make ready 300 g all-purpose flour
  2. Get 1/2 cup milk
  3. Get 1/4 cup yoghurt
  4. Take 1 tbsp melted ghee or butter
  5. Get 2 tsp sugar
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
  7. Get 1/2 tsp baking powder
  8. Take 1/4 tsp baking soda
  9. Make ready For the filling
  10. Make ready 2 large boiled mashed
  11. Make ready 2 green chilis, finely chopped
  12. Take 2 tbsp chopped fresh coriander
  13. Prepare 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  14. Get 1 tsp red Chilli flakes(to taste)
  15. Make ready 1 tsp amchur powder
  16. Get Salt1/2 tsp (to taste)
  17. Get Garnish
  18. Make ready Melted ghee or butter
  19. Prepare Chopped coriander
Steps to make üçõAloo Kulchaüçõ

(spicy potato stuffed naan):

  1. Mix the flour, sugar, salt, baking powder, and baking soda together in a large bowl. - Add the milk, yoghurt and ghee. Mix to a dough. Knead in the bowl for 2-3 minutes to ensure the dough is well mixed. - Cover the bowl and leave for 2 hour to allow the dough to relax.
  2. Mix the filling ingredients together in a bowl. Adjust the flavours if desired. - Turn the dough out and divide into 4 pieces. Knead each piece in your hand for 15-20 seconds then shape into a ball.
  3. Flour your worksurface. For each ball of dough, press and roll like a chapati. Place the filling in the centre of the disc. Pull the sides up to make parcel and place with the seam underneath.
  4. Now take the balls of dough and roll out gently to a round shape. - Place the kulcha on a hot pan for about 1 minute. Turn over and cook the other side for about 30 seconds. Turn over once more and continue to cook for at least another minute or until you can see steam coming off the kulcha. - Remove from the pan, brush with melted ghee and sprinkle with coriander.
  5. #Note - - You can also sprinkle the kulcha with nigella seeds before cooking if you wish.
