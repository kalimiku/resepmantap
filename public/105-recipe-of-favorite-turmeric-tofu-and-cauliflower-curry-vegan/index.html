The ingredients needed to make Turmeric Tofu and Cauliflower curry (vegan):
  1. Take 1 large (or 2 small) Cauliflower(s)
  2. Prepare Fresh coriander - as garnishing at the end
  3. Make ready 1 coconut milk carton (200ml)
  4. Make ready 300-350 gram firm Tofu
  5. Take 1 white onion
  6. Take 2 garlic coves
  7. Take 1 package mange tout (100-150 gram)
  8. Make ready 1 red pepper
  9. Take Coconut oil (1.5 table spoon)
  10. Prepare Handful coriander seeds
  11. Make ready 1 table spoon cumin seeds
  12. Get 1 table spoon turmeric
  13. Prepare 1 table spoon curry powder
  14. Prepare Pinch salt & pepper

Instructions to make Turmeric Tofu and Cauliflower curry (vegan):
  1. Chop the cauliflower, pepper, tofu in big pieces, Clean the mange tout. Put the oven on 180degrees.
  2. Put up rice (brown rice is my favourite - takes 30-45 min to cook depending on the rice you use)
  3. Add everything in a baketray and add 1 table spoon of coconut oil and the curry powder + turmeric + salt & pepper. Hustle and put in the oven for 25 min
  4. Add 0.5 table spoon oil in a deep pan, and fry the coriander seed + cumin seeds.
  5. After 2 minutes (and the seeds begin to pop) add cut onion + garlic (in small pieces).
  6. After 5-10 minutes (when onion is soft) add the coconut milk and put on low heat. Wait 10-15 minutes, then check the oven, if the veggies + tofu are well baked (soft/crispy on the outside) add them in the pan and cook together for 5 more minutes
  7. Drain the rice and serve all together - bon appetite :)
  8. Add fresh cut coriander (as in the picture) as finishing touch !
