The ingredients needed to make Spicy chilli vorta:
  1. Make ready 1 tsp Red chilli powder
  2. Make ready Few clover of garlic
  3. Make ready 1 tomato
  4. Get red onion,1 medium size
  5. Get lemon juice
  6. Prepare 1/3 tsp sugar
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  8. Prepare 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  9. Prepare 1/2 tsp curry powder
  10. Make ready 1/3 tsp cumin powder
  11. Make ready Salt and oil

Instructions to make Spicy chilli vorta:
  1. Heat oil (two tbsp) in a pan add chopped onion, garlic,tomato
  2. Add salt, cover and cook till tomato become soft and mushy
  3. Make a paste of dry spices with water,add this spice paste,mix everything well cook for 5min in low heat
  4. Add less then 1/2 cup water mix again
  5. Cook for 10 min in medium to low heat
  6. When oil separates from spices it’s cooked add lemon juice n sugar,copped coriander will give nice flavour to the vorta
