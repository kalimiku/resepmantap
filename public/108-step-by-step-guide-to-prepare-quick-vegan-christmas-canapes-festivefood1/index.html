The ingredients needed to make Vegan Christmas Canapes #festivefood1:
  1. Take 1 can boiled chick peas
  2. Take 1/2 cup green peas
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup butternut squash
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup spinach
  5. Get 2 medium size potatoes
  6. Get 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  7. Take 1/4 tsp chilli powder
  8. Prepare 1/4 tsp garam Masala powder
  9. Get 1 tbsp tomato paste
  10. Make ready 1/4 tsp panch phoron
  11. Get 2 tbsp olive oil
  12. Make ready to taste Salt
  13. Take 1/2 cup fresh coriander
  14. Take 1/4 tsp ginger and garlic paste
  15. Prepare 1 bay leaf
Instructions to make Vegan Christmas Canapes #festivefood1:
  1. Cut the butternut squash into small pieces.
  2. In a saucepan add oil, bay leaf, onion and panch phoron. Fry them for few minutes. Then add ginger and garlic paste. Add some water.
  3. Then mix all the spices and fry them.
  4. Add all the vegetables except spinach. Cook them for 10mins. Then add spinach.
  6. Boil the potatoes separately. Then mash them. Add 1/4 cup of fried onion and salt. Green chillies are optional. I have used here 1 green chilli.
  7. Once they are cooked turn off the gas. Then add the mash potato with them. I have made them in ball shapes. Then fry them either bread crumbs or flour. I have used here flour.
