The ingredients needed to make SOTO BETAWI KUAH SANTAN (SPICED SOUP):
  1. Get 5 tablespoons oil
  2. Get 3 salam leaves (or by leaves as a substitute)
  3. Take 5 cm cinnamon stick
  4. Take 1/2 nutmeg, bruised
  5. Prepare 500 gram brisket/ ribs
  6. Prepare 500 cc thick coconut milk
  7. Take 2 spring onions, finely sliced
  8. Get 2 sprigs Chinese parsley, finely chopped
  9. Get 2-3 tomatoes, cut into wedges
  10. Take 3 tablespoon fried shallots for garnish
  11. Get 1 lime juice, sliced to serve
  12. Take to taste Sweet Soy Sauce
  13. Prepare And crackers
  14. Take Spices and Ground :
  15. Prepare 6 cloves garlic
  16. Make ready 7 shallots
  17. Get 2 cm chopped ginger
  18. Take 2 cm chopped galangal
  19. Get 1 teaspoon coriander
  20. Make ready 1-2 teaspoon peppercorns
  21. Get 1 teaspoon cumin, roasted
  22. Get 5 candlenuts, roasted

Lalu santan diaduk-aduk agar tidak pecah dan tunggu sampai mendidih. Lalu masukkan gula, merica, dan garam. Soto Betawi dikenal dengan kuah santan dan kuah susu segar. Kuah susu segar merupakan adaptasi kuliner Arab, Smeentara yang berkuah santan merupakan kuliner lokal.

Steps to make SOTO BETAWI KUAH SANTAN (SPICED SOUP):
  1. Saute the ground spices, salam leaves, cinnamon stick and nutmeg until fragrant.
  2. Then add the brisket and stirr well. Pour in the stock and simmer over low heat until cooked.
  3. Add coconut milk, and continue to stir from time to time. And simmer over low heat until fragrant.
  4. Serve in a bowl, then add chinese parsley, spring onions and tomatoes. Garnish with a sprinkle of Fried shallots and pour the hot gravy over. Serve with sliced lime, sweet soy sauce, crackers and spicy sambal and warm rice.
