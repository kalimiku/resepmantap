The ingredients needed to make Spicy potato salad:
  1. Take 6 medium potatoes
  2. Prepare 3 tbls chopped gherkins
  3. Take 3 tbls sweet corn
  4. Make ready 2 green onions stalks (chopped)
  5. Get 1/2 cup light mayonnaise
  6. Get 3 tbls yellow mustard
  7. Take 1 tsp garlic paste
  8. Get 1 tbls paprika
  9. Take 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
  10. Make ready 1 tbls gherkin/pickle juice
  11. Prepare 1 tbls lemon juice
  12. Prepare to taste Salt and pepper
  13. Make ready 2 tbls chopped fresh mint leaves

Instructions to make Spicy potato salad:
  1. Dice the potatoes and boil them in salty water until cooked through. Drain and set aside to cool
  2. In a bowl, add the mayonnaise, mustard, pickle juice, lemon juice, garlic paste, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt & pepper and mix well to combine
  3. To the drained potatoes, add the sweet corn, gherkins and green onions. Mix until well combined
  4. Pour the mayonnaise mixture to the potato mixture until all the ingredients are completely mixed
  5. Refrigerate the potato salad before serving cool
