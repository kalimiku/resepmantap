The ingredients needed to make Spicy purple pickled eggs:
  1. Take 6 large eggs
  2. Prepare 400 ml vinegar
  3. Make ready 400 ml beet juice
  4. Get 2 jalapeños
  5. Make ready 20 g pickling spice
  6. Prepare 3 tbsp white sugar

Steps to make Spicy purple pickled eggs:
  1. Hard boil the eggs for 15-20mins, leave to cool. Once cooled, peel and leave to one side.
  2. Add beet juice, vinegar, sugar, pickling spice and jalapeños (chopped finely) to a pan. Heat until the point of boiling.
  3. Pour liquid over eggs in a glass storage container. Ready to eat in 24hrs and will keep for up to 2 weeks.
