The ingredients needed to make Spiced sweetcorn potato cakes:
  1. Take 500 g leftover mashed potato
  2. Take 5 tbsp sweetcorn
  3. Take 1/2 red onion finely diced
  4. Make ready 1 tbsp ground coriander
  5. Get 1 tbsp ground cumin
  6. Make ready 2 tbsp turmeric
  7. Prepare 2 tbsp chopped coriander
  8. Make ready 2 tbsp chopped mint
  9. Get 1/4 tsp chilli powder
  10. Prepare 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  11. Make ready 2 tsp ginger puree
  12. Get Handful chopped spinach
  13. Get Salt and pepper to season
  14. Prepare 1 small egg
  15. Get 30 g cornflour
  16. Make ready 1 tbsp oil

Steps to make Spiced sweetcorn potato cakes:
  1. Combine potato, spices, ginger, sweetcorn, finely diced onion, spinach and herbs in a mixing bowl.
  2. Beat the egg in a separate bowl.
  3. Use 1 tbsp of mixture and form a ball with your hands. Gently flatten.
  4. Dip into cornflour and egg. Place into pan with a little oil. Fry on low medium low heat for approximately 2 mins until golden then turn over.
  5. Serve warm with fresh sliced tomatoes and spinach leaves for a light meal.
