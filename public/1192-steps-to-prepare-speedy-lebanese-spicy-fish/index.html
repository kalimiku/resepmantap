Instructions to make Lebanese_spicy_fish:
  1. Clean fish and wash thoroughly. Strain and pat dry from excess water using some kitchen paper towels.
  2. Salt the fish & set aside for a few minutes so as salt can seep into the fish. Coat the fish with flour so it doesn‚Äôt stick or crumble during frying
  3. In a deep pot heat enough vegetable oil & deep fry the fish until its fully cooked then set aside.
  4. In a large saute pan add 3 tbsp of olive oil & saute the cilantro, garlic until aromatic and golden. Add the chili pepper, lemon juice & spices, stirring all ingredients & bring to a boil
  5. Finally add the fish and walnuts simmering for 20 minutes over heat.
  6. Transfer the fish onto your choice of serving dish,and garnish with fresh green coriander.
