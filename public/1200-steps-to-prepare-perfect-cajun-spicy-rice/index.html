The ingredients needed to make Cajun spicy rice:
  1. Make ready 300 g long grain rice
  2. Get 100 g frozen peas
  3. Make ready 100 g sweetcorn
  4. Make ready 1 tbsp oil
  5. Make ready 2 onions, diced
  6. Make ready 200 g minced beef or chicken
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp cajun spice
  8. Prepare 2 tbsp Worcester sauce
  9. Get 150 g peppers
  10. Make ready 200 ml beef stock
  11. Take 4 spring onions

  3. Add peppers, caujan spice and Worcester sauce
