The ingredients needed to make Pasta rustica piccante spicy garlic and herb pasta:
  1. Take 500 g pasta
  2. Make ready 2 cloves garlic chopped
  3. Prepare Good amount of fresh basil and parsley, finely chopped
  4. Take Olive oil
  5. Make ready Peperoncino - dried chilli
  6. Take Tin chopped tomatoes
  7. Prepare Pecorino cheese optional
  8. Prepare to taste Salt
