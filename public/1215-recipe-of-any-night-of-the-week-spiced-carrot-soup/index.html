Steps to make Spiced carrot soup:
  1. Rinse and boil lentils, removing froth
  2. Fry onion and garlic in olive oil until caramelised
  3. Chop carrots very chunky
  4. Add carrots, onion, garlic to the lentils.
  5. Grind the cumin, cardamom, coriander seeds and toast them, then add to lentils.
  6. Add everything else and simmer until the carrots are soft
  7. Food processor to make it into a puree, except some pieces of carrot
  8. Heat up and add water as needed for consistency, and taste for flavours and adjust.
  9. Serve with toast and a dollop of Greek yoghurt.
