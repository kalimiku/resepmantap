The ingredients needed to make Ginger Spiced Hot Chocolate:
  1. Make ready 250 ml dairy free milk
  2. Make ready 50 ml water
  3. Take 2 tbsps maple syrup
  4. Make ready 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
  5. Get 1/4 tsp ground ginger
  6. Take 1 tbsp cocoa powder

Instructions to make Ginger Spiced Hot Chocolate:
  1. Pour the milk and water into a saucepan over a medium heat - Bring to a gentle simmer then turn down the heat
  2. Whisk in the maple syrup, cinnamon, ginger and cocoa powder - Stir continuously until well combined
  3. Pour into a mug and top with some dairy free whipping cream and an extra sprinkle of cinnamon and ginger
