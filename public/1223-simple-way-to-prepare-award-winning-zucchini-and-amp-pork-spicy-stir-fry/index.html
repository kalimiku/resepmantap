The ingredients needed to make Zucchini & Pork Spicy Stir-fry:
  1. Prepare 1-2 Zucchini *sliced to 3 to 4mm thickness
  2. Take 1 clove Garlic *finely chopped
  3. Get 200-300 g Pork *thinly sliced
  4. Make ready Salt & Pepper
  5. Take Potato Starch Flour
  6. Take 1 tablespoon Vegetable Oil
  7. Prepare 1 teaspoon Sesame Oil
  8. Make ready <Sauce>
  9. Get 1 tablespoon Sugar
  10. Make ready 1 tablespoon Mirin
  11. Get 2 tablespoons Soy Sauce
  12. Take 1/2 tablespoon Toban Djan (Chili Bean Sauce)

Instructions to make Zucchini & Pork Spicy Stir-fry:
  1. Salt & Pepper to the sliced Pork, add some Potato Starch Flour and coat them well.
  2. Make the sauce by mixing all ingredients together.
  3. Heat Oils in a frying pan and cook Pork. When the pork changed colour, add Zucchini and Garlic and cook for a few minutes, but do not over cook.
  4. Add the sauce and combine everything well. Serve with freshly cooked rice.
