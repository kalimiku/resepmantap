Instructions to make Marinaded BBQ chicken thighs:
  1. Pierce the chicken thighs a few times with a sharp knife. This will help the marinade penetrate the meat.
  2. Mix the dry marinade ingredients in a large bowl and add the chicken thighs. Ensure they are coated well with the mixture, cover and leave in the fridge for 30 minutes
  3. Once the dry marinade has rested for 30 minutes, mix the wet marinade together and pour over the chicken thighs. Leave to marinade for at least 1 hour, preferably longer and ideally overnight. You may need to occasionally stir the chicken in the marinade to ensure it's absorbing all the flavour.
  4. Preheat the oven to 160¬∞c, 320¬∞f or gas mark 3 and cook the chicken for 45 mins, until cooked though and the juices run clear.
  5. Finish the chicken thighs on the BBQ for 15 mins to caramelise the marinade on the chicken. Baste with the remaining marinade from cooking.
  6. If you don't want to finish on the BBQ you can pop the chicken under the grill to achieve a similar effect.
