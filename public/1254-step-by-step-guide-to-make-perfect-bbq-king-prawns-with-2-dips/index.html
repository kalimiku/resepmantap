The ingredients needed to make BBQ King Prawns with 2 Dips:
  1. Prepare 15 King Prawns
  2. Make ready Honey and Soy Dip
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup Soy Sauce
  4. Make ready 2 Tbs runny Honey
  5. Get 1/2 tsp sesame oil
  6. Make ready Pinch chilly flakes
  7. Get Garlic, Lemon and Butter Dip
  8. Take 1/2 cup butter
  9. Make ready 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  10. Take 1 Tbsp Lemon juice
  11. Take 1 large garlic clove finely chopped
  12. Prepare 1/2 tsp Garlic powder
  13. Get 2 drops tobacco sauce
