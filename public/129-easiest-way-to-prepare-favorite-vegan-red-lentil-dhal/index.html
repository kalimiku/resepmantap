The ingredients needed to make Vegan Red Lentil Dhal:
  1. Make ready 275 g red lentils
  2. Get 1 onion, finely diced
  3. Get 3 garlic cloves, finely diced
  4. Make ready 2 tsp ginger, grated
  5. Get 2 tsp ground cumin
  6. Make ready 2 tsp turmeric
  7. Prepare 2 tsp garam masala
  8. Prepare 1/2 tsp paprika
  9. Make ready 1 tsp coriander, chopped
  10. Take to taste Salt
  11. Make ready 1 can coconut milk
  12. Make ready 500 ml vegetable stock
  13. Take 1 can chopped tomatoes
  14. Take 2 tbsp Olive oil
  15. Prepare Lemon juice, to garnish
  16. Make ready Chapatti bread, to serve
  17. Prepare Rice, to serve
  18. Make ready Spinach, to serve

Steps to make Vegan Red Lentil Dhal:
  1. Heat the olive oil over medium/high heat.
  2. Add the onion, garlic and ginger. Fry until golden brown.
  3. Add all the dried spices and cook for another minute or so. Stir regularly.
  4. Next add the tomatoes, vegetable stock, coconut milk and lentils.
  5. Reduce heat to medium and cook for 15 minutes or until the mixture has thickened.
  6. Add the coriander and season with salt to taste.
  7. Serve the dhal with rice, spinach and chapatti bread. Garnish the dhal with a drizzle of lemon juice just before serving or plate a slice of lemon to be drizzled over.
