The ingredients needed to make Salmon Thai Burgers:
  1. Prepare For the burgers:
  2. Make ready 200 g salmon fillet, skin removed
  3. Make ready 25 g gluten free breadcrumbs
  4. Make ready 1 tablespoons mayonnaise
  5. Get 2-3 teaspoons red Thai curry paste
  6. Take 1 teaspoon coriander leaf
  7. Make ready 1 teaspoon freshly grated ginger
  8. Take 1 garlic clove, minced
  9. Get salt and pepper, for seasoning
  10. Take To serve:
  11. Make ready 2 gluten free rolls
  12. Make ready spinach
  13. Get gluten free onion rings (I picked some up in Tesco)
  14. Take mayonnaise
  15. Get sweet chilli sauce
  16. Get sliced tomatoes
Instructions to make Salmon Thai Burgers:
  1. Place the salmon, breadcrumbs, mayo, curry paste, ginger, garlic, coriander and seasoning into a food processor - Shape into 2 patties and place into the freezer for 15 minutes
  2. Heat a splash of olive oil in a shallow pan over a medium heat - Cook for about 4 minutes on each side
  3. Serve with fries in a bun with chilli sauce, onion rings, mayo, tomatoes and spinach
