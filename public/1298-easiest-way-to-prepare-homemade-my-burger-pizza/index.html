The ingredients needed to make My burger pizza:
  1. Prepare 1 frozen large cheese pizza
  2. Prepare 2 frozen 1/4 pounder burgers
  3. Make ready 1 cup mature red cheddar
  4. Get 1 tomato sliced
  5. Take 1 tsp red chilli flakes
  6. Make ready to taste Sriracha sauce

Steps to make My burger pizza:
  1. Put burgers on a tray. Then put pizza with half cup of cheese on another tray in oven at 170°c
  2. After 10 minutes take out pizza and burgers top with tomato and sliced burger
  3. Top with cheese and cook until golden brown
  4. Once cooked slice into 4 slices and drizzle with sriracha sauce
