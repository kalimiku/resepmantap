The ingredients needed to make Chicken and Beef Skewers:
  1. Prepare Chicken Marinade
  2. Make ready 4-5 chicken breasts, cut into bite size cubes
  3. Take 2 tsp garlic, crushes
  4. Get 1 tsp ground garlic
  5. Prepare 1 chicken stock cube
  6. Make ready 1 Tbsp mixed herbs
  7. Make ready 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  8. Take 1 tsp all spice
  9. Prepare 1/4 cup natural or greek yoghurt
  10. Get Juice of 1 lemon
  11. Make ready Beef Marinade
  12. Make ready 500 g beef, cubed
  13. Get 3 tsp garlic, crushed
  14. Take 1 Tbsp all purpose seasoning
  15. Make ready 1 Tbsp dried basil
  16. Prepare 1 tsp sugar
  17. Prepare 1 Tbsp paprika
  18. Get 1 beef stock cube
  19. Take 1 tsp sugar
  20. Prepare 1 Tbsp white wine vinegar
  21. Take 1 Tbsp olive oil
  22. Take What you will need
  23. Take 1 cup melted butter
  24. Take 2 bell peppers, red & green, cut into large chunks
  25. Get 14 wooden skewers
  26. Prepare 2 bowls
Instructions to make Chicken and Beef Skewers:
  1. Chicken marinade - in a bowl, mix all chicken marinade ingredients. Add the chicken to the marinade and mix well coating all chicken pieces. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.

  3. Thread the chicken and peppers onto skewers. Do the same for the beef.
  4. Grill for 8-10 minutes, turning them frequently and baste with the melted butter frequently.
  5. Serve with a salad and sauce to dunk in.
