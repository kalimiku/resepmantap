The ingredients needed to make Tamarind juice:
  1. Make ready 10 fingers of Tamarind
  2. Get 1/4 cup sugar
  3. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  4. Get 2 cups water
  5. Get 6 pieces cube ice block
  6. Prepare 1/2 lemon

Steps to make Tamarind juice:
  1. Peel off tamarind and wash it
  2. Put tamarind into a pot and add water, set to fire to boil
  3. Remove from heat and let it cool for 5mins, sieve in a bowl using a sieve
  4. Add sugar, vanilla extract,and squeeze lemon in it
  5. Pour out your mixture into 2 glasses cup, divide ice block into 2, put to the mixture and serve
