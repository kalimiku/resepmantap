  7. Take 3 of garlic

Instructions to make Grandpa Sate / Grandpa Kebab:
  1. Mix and knead pork with ground spices, sweet soy sauce and oil. Let the mixture stand at least 15 minutes.
  2. Thread 6-8 pieces pork into skewer, brush it with remaining marinade and grill until golden brown.
  3. Serve with sweet soy sauce or peanut sauce, or both.
  4. For the sweet soy sauce: Grind some bird's eye chillies with shallots and combine with sweet soy sauce.
  5. For the Peanut Sauce: Grind red chilli with roasted fried peanut. Add water and stir well.
  6. Serve with rice or with cake rice or just eat like that.
