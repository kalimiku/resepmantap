The ingredients needed to make Cheese Burger In A Bowl:
  1. Make ready 100-160 g 7% fat turkey thigh mince, (or alternative),
  2. Get 80 g iceberg lettuce, sliced or shredded,
  3. Take 1 tomato, sliced into 8ths,
  4. Take Half a small red onion, sliced into thin pieces,
  5. Prepare 2 pickled cucumbers/gerkins, sliced into rings, (optional),
  6. Take 1/2 tbsp burger sauce,
  7. Take 20 g 50% reduced fat cheddar, grated,
  8. Get 1/4 tsp garlic granules,
  9. Take Salt and pepper to season
  10. Take Spray cooking oil for frying

Instructions to make Cheese Burger In A Bowl:
  1. Heat up a frying pan over a medium to high heat. Spray oil in to cover the base then once warm add in the turkey mince. Fry and brown off, season with salt and pepper and the garlic granules. Stir together, and fry until cooked through. Turn off the heat and leave the frying pan on the hob to keep warm.
  2. In a large shallow bowl add in the shredded lettuce. Add the sliced onion and tomato. Also add the pickles.
  3. Top with the warm turkey mince and scatter over the grated cheese. Dress with burger sauce. Eat and enjoy!
