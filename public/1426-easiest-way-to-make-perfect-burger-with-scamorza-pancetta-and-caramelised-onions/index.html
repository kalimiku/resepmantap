Steps to make Burger with scamorza, pancetta and caramelised onions:
  1. Fry the pancetta, set a side. In the same pan, fry onions with a little sugar. Cook until looking yummy. Set a side
  2. Get cheese ready. To your taste, mix garlic a mayo. Set a side
  3. Cook your burgers how ever you like, grilled or fried. Slice buns and put in the oven or under the grill for 5 mins to crisp up
  4. When the burgers are cooked, add cheese on top, let it melt for a min or so. Start to plate up. Add burger to the bun
  5. Add onions. Add pancetta. Add mayo. Serve with fries or chips :)
