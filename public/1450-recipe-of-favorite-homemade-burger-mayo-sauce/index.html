The ingredients needed to make Homemade Burger Mayo sauceüçî:
  1. Get Mayonnaise (1/2 cup)
  2. Make ready Vinegar (2 tsp)
  3. Take Red chilli flakes (1 tsp)
  4. Take Tomato ketchup (2 tbsp)
  5. Get Sugar (1/2 tsp)
  6. Prepare Salt (1/2 tsp)
  7. Make ready 1 leaf Coriander for garnish

Instructions to make Homemade Burger Mayo sauceüçî:
  1. In a bowl add mayonnaise and whisk with fork until light and airy. Then add vinegar and mix well until smooth. After that add red chilli flakes and mix well.
  2. Now add tomato ketchup and mix well. When mixed sprinkle salt and sugar. Now coat well until smooth.
  3. Garnish with a leaf if coriander. All done. Serve cold with your burger or sandwich. Enjoy.
