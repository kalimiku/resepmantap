The ingredients needed to make Chicken Chapli Kebab ☺:
  1. Prepare Chicken breast chopped (1 kg)
  2. Make ready Tomato (1 medium chopped)
  3. Make ready Green chilli (1 large chopped)
  4. Get Mint leaves (1 cup)
  5. Get Onion (1 medium cut in 4 portions)
  6. Prepare Garlic paste (1 tbsp)
  7. Prepare Egg (1)
  8. Prepare Oil (1/2 cup)
  9. Make ready Chapli kebab curry powder (2 tbsp)
  10. Make ready Coriander for garnish

Steps to make Chicken Chapli Kebab ☺:
  1. Cut the chicken breast.
  2. Chop the tomato.
  3. Arrange the ingredients required.
  4. Chapli kebab curry powder.
  5. In a food processor add chicken.
  6. Green chillis, onion, and garlic paste.
  7. Egg, mint leaves and chapli kebab powder. Now process well.
  8. Transfer it on to a plastic bowl.
  9. In a wok add oil. Heat it up. Then add tomato.
  10. Mix and mash the tomato on low flame.
  11. Add chicken kebab mixture.
  12. Cook until white on high flame.
  13. Then cook the kebab mixture on low flame until golden brown.
  14. When done garnish with coriander and serve hot with rice or flat bread. Enjoy ☺
