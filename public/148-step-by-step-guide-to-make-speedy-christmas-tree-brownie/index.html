The ingredients needed to make Christmas tree brownie:
  1. Prepare 1/4 cup butter
  2. Take 1/2 cup milkmaid
  3. Get 1/2 cup sugar
  4. Take 1 tablespoon vanilla essence
  5. Get 1/2 cup milk
  6. Make ready 1 cup refined flour
  7. Get 1/4 cup coco powder
  8. Prepare 1 teaspoon baking powder
  9. Get 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  10. Prepare 50 gms dark compound
  11. Prepare 50 gms white colour
  12. Prepare 60 gms fresh cream
  13. Take as required Sprinkles

Steps to make Christmas tree brownie:
  1. Take a bowl and add butter, milkmaid, sugar, vanilla essence and milk and mix it well.
  2. Add sifted maida and cocoa powder.
  3. Add baking soda and baking powder and mix slowly.
  4. Take an aluminum square-shaped tin and apply oil to it. After applying oil add the batter into tin
  5. Preheat the oven at 180¬∞c and bake it for 30 minutes.
  6. After 30 minutes check the brownie with a toothpich, if the toothpick is clean it means your brownies are ready.
  7. Let the brownies cool down completely.
  8. Cut the brownies into 2 halves
  9. Cut into triangles and melt the dark chocolate and cream together and once it gets good black and thick apply to brownie
  10. Melt white chocolate and add green edible colour to it and mix it well. Add this to piping bag and sprinkle on the brownies
  11. Once the tree is done with sprinkling chocolate and some stars and sprinkles on brownies
  12. For Christmas stem place printed straw. Your brownies are ready to eat
