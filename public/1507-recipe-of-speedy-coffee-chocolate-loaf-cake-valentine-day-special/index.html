Steps to make Coffee chocolate loaf cake 👉 valentine day special 🍫:
  1. In a mixing bowl sieve all dry ingredients keep it a side
  2. In a other mixing bowl add all given wet ingredients beat it well
  3. Now take a dry ingredients bowl add grated chocolate, wet ingredients little by little mix it properly make a smooth flowing consistency batter
  4. Now take a loaf cake mould put butter pepper grease with oil then pour prepared batter now tap twice remove it bubbles
  5. Now bake it preheated oven at 180c for 20
  6. Now take out microwave and demould it ad cut in to pices and through some sugar powder and serve it
