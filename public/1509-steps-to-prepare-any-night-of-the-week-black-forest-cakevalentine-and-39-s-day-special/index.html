  2. Make ready 1 cup sugar powder
  3. Take 1 cup all purpose flour
  4. Get 1/2 tsp baking powder
  5. Take 2 pinches baking soda
  6. Take 1/2 cup vegetable oil

  8. Prepare 1 pinch salt

  10. Get 1/2 cup dark chocolate shavings
  11. Make ready Some cherries for garnishing

  1. Set the tin for baking with parchment paper.
  2. Sieve dry ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Take 3 eggs white and start beating by electric beater.
  4. Egg white looking fluffy then add egg yolks. Now add dry ingredients in egg white
  5. Cake batter is ready. Preheated the oven for 10 minutes.
  6. Use wooden stick in between cake sponge is ready. Now make a sugar syrup for sponge.
  7. Now whipped the heavy whipping cream
