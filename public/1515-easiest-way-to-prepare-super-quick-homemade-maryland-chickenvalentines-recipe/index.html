Instructions to make Maryland chicken#valentines recipe:
  1. Boil the chicken in salted water till tender
  2. Mix the bread crumbs with spices.
  3. Beat the eggs in a bowl
  4. Pat the chicken dry witha paper towel then coat with flour, shake off excess flour, dip in egg then finally in the bread crumbs repeat the process with all pieces.
