The ingredients needed to make Valentine's Day special "Tava Paneer Tikka":
  1. Make ready 200 gm Paneer
  2. Get 1 cup Yoghurt /curd
  3. Get 1/2 Capsicum
  4. Prepare 1 Onion
  5. Take 1 Tomato
  6. Get 1 Lemon
  7. Make ready 3-4 Garlic slices chopped
  8. Get to taste Salt according
  9. Make ready 2 tbsp Red chilli powder
  10. Make ready 2 tbsp Tandoori powder
  11. Take 1 small Garlic and coriander paste bowl
  12. Get 2 tbsp Cumin powder
  13. Take 3 tbsp Besan
  14. Make ready 1 tbsp Mustard oil
  15. Get Carrot 1 (small)
  16. Make ready 250 gm Butter
Instructions to make Valentine's Day special "Tava Paneer Tikka":
  1. Ek bdey bowl mein 2 big spoon curd lein agr yoghurt available hai toh wo b lein sktey hain ab usme saare masale, chopped garlic, garlic and coriander ka paste milaye or achchey so mix krein
  2. Ab usi mixture mein besan or mustard oil or cumin powder b daalein or phir usme paneer kaat ke daalein (paneer ko bdey bdey cubes mein cut krein)
  3. Ab is marinate ko 1.5 hrs ke liye fridge mein rkhdein or marinate ki consistency thick rkhein
  4. Ab 1.5 hrs baad marinate ko nikalein or phir ek Pan pe achchey se butter daalein or achchey so spread krein
  5. Ab marinated paneer ko tavey pe fry krein wahi dusri taraf sari vegetables ko b achchey se butter mein fry kar lein.
  6. Apka tava paneer tikka tyar.Ab isme nimbu daal kar kuch aise serve krein green chutney ke saath.
