Instructions to make Blueberry cake #my valentines recipe:
  1. Mix the margarine and sugar until light and fluffy.
  2. Add the eggs, milk, and oil and whisk
  3. Add the flour(baking powder incorporated) bit by bit as you whisk. The butter need to be thick
  4. Coat the blue berries with flour and add to the butter
  5. In a preheated oven at 180 degrees put the cake mixtures. Bake until the cake is ready ie when you insert a tooth pick it comes out clean
  6. Serve with beverage of choice
