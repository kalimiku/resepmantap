The ingredients needed to make Valentine Icecream:
  1. Make ready 1 glass Heavy fat milk
  2. Prepare 1 bowl Cream
  3. Prepare 1/2 bowl Powdered Sugar
  4. Take 1/2 teaspoon Vanila essence
  5. Make ready 1 tsp Cornflour
  6. Prepare 1 big bowl Ice container
  7. Take 4/5 Oreo biscuits
  8. Get 2/3 Strawberries for decoration
  9. Get 2 tea spoon Roohaafza syrup for garnishing and decoration
  10. Get 2 teaspoon Khush syrup for garnishing and decoration
  11. Get Few chocolate (optional) for decoration
  12. Take Whipping electric blender
Instructions to make Valentine Icecream:
  1. Take heavy milk in a pan and bring it to a boil.
  2. In a cup take corn flour and add 2 t spoon milk, mix well so dat no lumps r seen.
  3. Once milk is boiling add the cornflour mixer and continuously stirring boil it 5/6 mins on slow to medium flame.
  4. Allow the pan to cool down on room temperature.
  5. In a bowl take cream and place it above big container hvg ice cubes. Now whip the cream for 2/3 mins. Add vanilla essence and again whip for 1 min. Add powdered sugar and whip for 2 more mins.
  6. Add milk to the cream and whip for 2 mins.
  7. Pour the liquid icecream in icecream container and place it in freezer and let it freeze for 2 hrs.
  8. Remove frm freezer. Icecream is partially frozen. Whip again for 2 mins. Now finally place in freezer for 6/7 hrs.
  9. Vanila icecream is ready
  10. Now place the ice-cream scoop in icecream glasses.
  11. Take oreo biscuits, crush dem and sprinkle on the ice-cream scoop.
  12. Take roohaafza syrup and pour on the scoop.
  13. Take khush syrup and pour on the scoop.
  14. Syrup sud the poured in such a way dat icecream sud get nic catchy and decorative luk.
  15. Place oreo biscuits in each glass.
  16. Place Strawberries by cutting in Heart shape and place in middle of both the glasses, one upon another to get dat Valentine feeling. üòç
  17. Now place the glasses in ne plate and decorate the plate vth oreo biscuits,strawberry, roohaafza syrup, khush syrup and Chocolates which r always complimenting Valentine Day.
