  1. Take bread slices 5
  2. Make ready cheese cube 2

  4. Make ready 4 tbsp capcicum finely chopped
  5. Prepare 4 tbsp onion finely chopped
  6. Get 3 tbsp pizza sauce
  7. Take as needed tomato sauce
  8. Take 2 tbsp butter
  9. Get 1 tsp origano

Instructions to make Noodle pizza hearts…valentines special.:
  1. Cut the bread slices with the help of heart shape cutter
  2. Mix all the veggies in a bowl
  3. Add pizza sauce to the veggies
  4. Mix boil noodles in it
  5. Spread little butter on bread hearts
  6. Place filling over them
  7. Add grated cheese and place over the pan on low flame for 1min
  8. Cheese get melted turn off the gas
  9. Garnish with sauce sprinkle some origano chilli flakes and serve..
