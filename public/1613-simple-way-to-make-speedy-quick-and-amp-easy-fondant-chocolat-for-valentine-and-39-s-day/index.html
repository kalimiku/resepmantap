The ingredients needed to make Quick & Easy Fondant Chocolat for Valentine's Day:
  1. Make ready 2 bars (55 g each) Chocolate bar
  2. Get 90 grams Butter (unsalted)
  3. Get 3 tbsp Cake flour
  4. Get 1 tbsp Cocoa powder
  5. Get 4 tbsp Caster sugar or granulated sugar
  6. Take 3 Eggs
  7. Take 1 dash Powdered sugar (Non-melting type)
