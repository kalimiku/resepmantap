The ingredients needed to make For Valentine's Day – Super Easy Chocolates:
  1. Take 20 large Marshmallows
  2. Get 1 Chocolate bar
  3. Take 1 i used 60 grams of meiji brand's chocolate bar
  4. Take 1 same number as marshmallows Toothpicks
Steps to make For Valentine's Day – Super Easy Chocolates:
  1. Stick the toothpicks into the marshmallows.
  2. Using a toothpick, poke holes in a Styrofoam board.
  3. Break the chocolate bar into chunks and place in a microwave safe bowl, and microwave (1.5 minutes at 600W)!
  4. Dip the marshmallows in the chocolate and stick into the Styrofoam. When the chocolate is set, it's done!
  5. Place in a serving container of your choice!
