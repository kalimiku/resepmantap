The ingredients needed to make Vegetarian tomato sauce tofu素食版西红柿烧豆腐:
  1. Take 2 tofu
  2. Prepare 3 tomatoes
  3. Get 100 g green peas
  4. Take 3 ginger slices
  5. Take 3 garlic cloves
  6. Take 1 small 🥄 of salt

Instructions to make Vegetarian tomato sauce tofu素食版西红柿烧豆腐:
  1. Cut tofu and tomatoes into small cubes. Chop ginger finely and cut garlic into slices.
  2. Boil tofu cubes in a pot, add one spoon of salt. Drain after tofu is cooked.
  3. Stir fry garlic and ginger then add tomatoes in, keep stir frying to make tomato sauce. I didn’t add any water.
  4. Add tofu and green peas and gently stir a bit. Add some salt, done✅
