The ingredients needed to make Braised Tofu w veges:

  2. Prepare Handful snow peas
  3. Take 1 carrot sliced
  4. Get 3 inch bamboo shoot sliced
  5. Make ready 5 shitaki mushrooms sliced
  6. Make ready 4 green scallions chopped
  7. Prepare 2 garlic cloves chopped

  9. Prepare 5/6 baby corn halved

  11. Make ready 2/3 cup warm water
  12. Prepare mix together, 1/2 tsp salt, 1/2 tsp sugar, 1/4 tsp pepper, 2 tsp oyster sauce, 1 tsp light soy, 1/4 tsp dark soy and 1 tsp sesame oil
  13. Take 1 tbsp oil
  14. Take 2 tsp corn starch mixed in 1/4 cup water
