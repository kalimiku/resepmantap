The ingredients needed to make Mapo tofu:
  1. Make ready 1 block soft or medium soft tofu (cut up into cubes)
  2. Prepare 2 tbsp corn flour (optional)
  3. Get 200 g mince beef or pork or chicken
  4. Take 2 tsp soysauce
  5. Make ready 2 tsp grated ginger (or ginger powder)
  6. Take 1 tsp sugar
  7. Prepare 1 tsp sesame oil
  8. Take 1 spring onions (finely chopped)
  9. Prepare 1 clove garlic (finely chopped)
  10. Make ready 2 tbsp vegetable cooking oil
  11. Take Ground black pepper
  12. Prepare 1 tsp spicy Bean paste (optional)
  13. Make ready 2 tsp chilli oil (can check my recipe)
