The ingredients needed to make Mapo Tofu:
  1. Get 2 tbsp chopped ginger
  2. Get 2 tbsp chopped garlic
  3. Get 150 g minced pork
  4. Prepare 2 tbsp chopped leek
  5. Prepare Salt and Pepper
  6. Get 1 packed Tofu
  7. Prepare 1.5 tbsp corn starch with 1.5 tbsp water
  8. Prepare 1 tbsp sesame oil
  9. Make ready Chili flakes
  10. Get 1 tsp sliced leek
  11. Make ready For the sauce
  12. Prepare 150 ml water
  13. Take 2 tbsp soy sauce
  14. Make ready 2 tbsp miso
Steps to make Mapo Tofu:
  1. In a frying pan - Put the olive oil, the chopped ginger and the garlic with low heat until it smells good.
  2. Add the minced pork and the chopped leek in the frying pan and heat until the pork becomes brown.
  3. Add a pinch of salt and pepper until taste is good.
  4. Cut the Tofu into 1.5cm cube.
  5. Add the Tofu, water, soy sauce and miso in the frying pan and boil for a while.
  6. In a cup - Mix the corn starch and water together.
  7. Turn off the heat of frying pan. Add the corn starch and water in the frying pan little by little and mix.
  8. Taste and adjust with salt. You can add chili flakes.
  9. Add sesame oil and the slice of leek and Enjoy!
