The ingredients needed to make Tofu scrambled on toast with sautéed mushrooms:
  1. Get Half block soft tofu
  2. Get 2 tbsp dashi stock
  3. Make ready 1 tsp turmeric powder
  4. Prepare 1 tsp paprika
  5. Get 1 spring onion (finely chopped)
  6. Get Salt and pepper
  7. Make ready 1-2 slices brown bread
  8. Make ready 1 handful mushrooms (thinly sliced)

Steps to make Tofu scrambled on toast with sautéed mushrooms:
  1. Mash up tofu in a mixing bowl or saucepan.
  2. Add some dashi stock, turmeric, salt and pepper.
  3. In a medium heat small saucepan add tofu meat in and mix well and stir for a few min. Add some paprika powder in mix well.
  4. Sautéed your mushrooms and seasoning with salt and pepper. Serve your tofu scrambled top with spring onion and mushrooms on brown toast
