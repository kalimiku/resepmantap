The ingredients needed to make Christmas baby choco cake:
  1. Take 1 packet bourbon biscuit
  2. Get 1 small packet oreo
  3. Make ready 4 tsp sugar
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup milk
  5. Prepare 2 tsp cashew
  6. Prepare 2 tsp cadbury games
  7. Make ready 2 tsp raisin
  8. Make ready 2 tsp crush almond
  9. Make ready 1 packet eno
  10. Get as needed butter paper
  11. Prepare 4 tsp butter

Instructions to make Christmas baby choco cake:
  1. At first,take the both biscuit and break into small pieces
  2. The biscuits have own cream and sugar
  3. Make powder from both biscuits and sugar.
  4. Then,mix all of them in a bowl.
  5. Put the milk on the powder biscuit and make a batter
  6. Add a eno in it and whisk
  7. Whisk the batter during 5min
  8. Make a smooth and creamy batter.
  9. Chop all dry fruits into small pieces
  10. Take a bowl with grease butter.
  11. Put butter paper in it.
  12. Grease butter on it once again.
  13. Keep the batter in it and spread equaliy to the edges in the bowl.
  14. Garnish with dry fruit
  15. Take any kind pot with water.
  16. Put it on gas with stand for boil,
  17. Put the mixture bowl in it.
  18. Cover it with a lid
  19. Then,after 20min check the position by the toothpeak
  20. When your toothpeak come out dry.
  21. Then,your cake is ready.
  22. Turn off the gas and take the bowl from pot after some time
  23. Take a plate and cover the bowl.
  24. Turn both of them and remove the cake from bowl.
  25. Turn the cake in a plate and garnish with cherry and games on it.
