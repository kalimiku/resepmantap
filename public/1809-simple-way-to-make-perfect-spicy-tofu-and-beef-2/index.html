The ingredients needed to make Spicy Tofu and Beef 2:
  1. Get 350 g (1 pack) hard tofu
  2. Make ready 100 g minced beef
  3. Prepare 15 cm spring onion
  4. Make ready leek
  5. Take 1 piece garlic
  6. Prepare 1 piece ginger
  7. Make ready 1 teaspoon Chinese chicken paste or chicken cube
  8. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
  9. Get 1/2 teaspoon Japanese pepper(Sansho pepper)
  10. Get to taste salt
  11. Get 100 ml water+2 teaspoon starch
Instructions to make Spicy Tofu and Beef 2:
  1. Cut tofu and vegetables.
  2. Fry garlic and ginger with vegetable oil. Then add beef and spring onion.
  3. Add tofu and seasonings. Put starch water and cook well and salt to taste.
  4. Put leek.
  5. Sprinkle chili oil. Enjoy ❣️
