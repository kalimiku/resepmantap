The ingredients needed to make Caipo Tofu:
  1. Make ready 1 block tofu (for frying)
  2. Make ready Preserved turnips (caipo)
  3. Get 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  4. Get 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  5. Get 4 tablespoon water
  6. Get 1 teaspoon shallots
  7. Prepare 1 teaspoon garlic
  8. Make ready Sugar (to taste)

Steps to make Caipo Tofu:
  1. Fill the pot with oil
  2. Deep fry the tofu and set aside
  3. Deep fry the caipo until they are light golden and crispy and set aside
  4. Pour away most of the oil until the pan seem lightly glazed
  5. With low heat, sweat the shallots, sesame oil and garlic until the shallots turns transparent
  6. Mix the soy sauce and water and add to the mixture
  7. Add sugar to the sauce to taste, approximately, 2-3 teaspoon
  8. Plate the tofu. If you like you can blanch vegetables and set on the side
