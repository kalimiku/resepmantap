The ingredients needed to make Fried Tofu With Spicy Garlic Sauce:
  1. Take 500 g Fresh Momen Tofu *OR Tofu of your choice
  2. Make ready Potato Starch Flour
  3. Take Oil for frying
  4. Take <Sauce>
  5. Make ready 1 clove Garlic *grated
  6. Prepare 1-2 Spring Onions *finely chopped
  7. Get 2 tablespoon Soy Sauce
  8. Make ready 1 tablespoon Rice Vinegar
  9. Make ready 1 tablespoon Sugar
  10. Make ready 1 tablespoon Lemon Juice
  11. Prepare 2-3 teaspoons Chilli Garlic Sauce
Instructions to make Fried Tofu With Spicy Garlic Sauce:
  1. Place Tofu on slightly tilted cutting board or plate with a flat-base dish or board as a weight on top, and set aside for 1/2 hour so that excess water would be removed. - *Note: If you use firm Tofu, you don‚Äôt need to remove water.
  2. Combine all the sauce ingredients in a small bowl.

  4. Place fried Tofu in a bowl and cover with the sauce.
