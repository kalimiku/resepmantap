Instructions to make Pork, Chinese Mushroom and Chili Tofu:
  1. Prepare some tofu, chili, spring onion, coriander, mushroom, pork belly and garlic.
  2. Cut the tofu into pieces then put into boiling water to boil until you can see some little holes on them. (very important step which can make tofu absorb more flavor later)
  3. Finely slice the pork belly and put into the pan (pan on low heat setting and with no oil), let the pork fat cook until the pork belly is crispy. Add the star anise, garlic and also the sliced mushroom. Once the mushrooms are cooked add some soy sauce and the boiled tofu, and water to cover the tofu. Leave to cook until broth has reduced.
