The ingredients needed to make Chicken & Tofu Burgers with Teriyaki Sauce:
  1. Make ready 500 g Chicken Mince
  2. Take 400-500 g Firm Tofu *mashed
  3. Take 1 Onion *optional, finely chopped
  4. Take 1 small piece Ginger *grated
  5. Take 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  6. Get 1/2 teaspoon White Pepper
  7. Make ready Oil for cooking
  8. Take Toasted Sesame Seeds
  9. Get <Sauce>
  10. Get 2 tablespoon Brown Sugar *OR White Sugar
  11. Get 2 tablespoon Mirin
  12. Make ready 4 tablespoons Soy Sauce
  13. Get 1/4 cup Sake (Rice Wine) or Water
  14. Take 1/2 tablespoons Potato Starch OR Corn Starch *mixed with 1 tablespoon Water
  15. Take 1 tablespoon Rice Vinegar

Steps to make Chicken & Tofu Burgers with Teriyaki Sauce:
  1. Combine all ingredients well in a bowl. Roll mixture into balls. Flatten slightly.
  2. Heat small amount of Oil in a frying pan and cook until golden and cooked though.
  3. Place Sugar, Mirin, Soy Sauce, Sake (Rice Wine) and Rice Vinegar in the cleaned frying pan OR saucepan, and bring it to the boil. Mix Potato Starch with Water and gradually add to the sauce, stirring, and cook until the sauce thickens.
  4. Combine ‘Chicken & Tofu Burgers’ with the sauce. I sprinkled with some Toasted Sesame Seeds.
