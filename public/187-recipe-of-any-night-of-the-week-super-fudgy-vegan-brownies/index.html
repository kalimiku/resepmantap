The ingredients needed to make Super-Fudgy vegan Brownies:
  1. Get 240 g kidney beans
  2. Take 60 g dates
  3. Prepare 25 g cocoa powder
  4. Make ready 200 g almond yoghurt
  5. Prepare 30 g oat flour
  6. Get 20 g vegan chocolate protein powder (or 10+10g more oat flour & cocoa powder)
  7. Prepare 30 g erythrit
  8. Take 1 pack baking powder
  9. Prepare For the topping:
  10. Prepare 100 ml almond yoghurt
  11. Make ready 10 g cocoa powder

These vegan brownies are super fudgy and moist. If you like your brownies ultra gooey you can make them like that too. But this isn't just to sweeten the brownies, it also creates that super fudgy and soft texture that we really want here. The BEST Vegan Brownies I've ever had!

Instructions to make Super-Fudgy vegan Brownies:
  1. Preheat the oven to 160° Celsius
  2. Drain and rinse the beans very well.
  3. Blend the beans, 100ml almond yoghurt and the dates into a purée.
  4. Stir in the oat flour, the protein powder, 15g cocoa powder, erythrit to form a sticky dough
  5. Spoon the dough in baking sheet lined tin & bake for 40 minutes
  6. Let it cool down, while prepare the topping. Mix 100ml almond yoghurt + 10g cocoa powder
  7. Cut the brownie and spoon the topping over them….
