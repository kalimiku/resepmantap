  4. Get 200 courgette or other squash
  5. Prepare 225 g smoked tofu
  6. Get 250 ml soya cream
  7. Take 1 tablespoon bouillon powder
  8. Get 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast
  9. Make ready 40 g peas
