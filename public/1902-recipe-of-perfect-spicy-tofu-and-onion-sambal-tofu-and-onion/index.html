The ingredients needed to make Spicy Tofu And Onion /Sambal Tofu And Onion:
  1. Get 4 square Pre Fried Tofu
  2. Prepare 3 large onion
  3. Make ready 2 tbsp Spicy sauce / sambal sauce or sambal oelek
  4. Take 1 tbsp light soy sauce
  5. Prepare 2 tsp brown sugar

Steps to make Spicy Tofu And Onion /Sambal Tofu And Onion:
  1. With oil deep fry the pre Fried Tofu for 2 minutes till it's Lightly brown then drain and set aside
  2. With 2 tbsp of oil pan fry the onion for about 1 minute then add in the Sambal oelek and stir to mix
  3. Add in the light soy sauce and brown sugar and continue to stir fry for another 1 minutes then pour onto the cook Tofu and serve
  4. Always goes well with white rice
