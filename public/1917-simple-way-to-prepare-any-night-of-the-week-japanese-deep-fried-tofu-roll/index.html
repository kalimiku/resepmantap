The ingredients needed to make Japanese Deep-fried Tofu Roll:
  1. Get 200 g pork- beef minced
  2. Prepare 8 pieces Aburaage (thin deep-fried bean curd Tofu)
  3. Get 2 tablespoon Okara(byproduct of Tofu making)
  4. Take 1 egg
  5. Take 1 carrot
  6. Get 1 pack mushroom
  7. Take Leek
  8. Make ready 1 teaspoon Dashi powder (Japanese fish bouillon powder)
  9. Prepare 100 ml seasoning Sake
  10. Get 100 ml Mirin Japanese seasoning sweet Sake
  11. Get 50 ml soy sauce
  12. Take 1 teaspoon sugar

Instructions to make Japanese Deep-fried Tofu Roll:
  1. Ingredients Aburaage. Thin deep-fried Tofu
  2. Cut three edges and open
  4. Mix meat and Okara and Dashi powder.
  5. Mix egg with hand.
  6. Spread meat on Aburaage
  7. Put shred carrot and chopped mushrooms and cut leen on it.
  9. Roll tight
  10. Seal the end with flour-water. And fry
  11. Mix Sake, Mirin, soy sauce and sugar and pour the mixed sauce in pan.
  12. Cover with cooking paper and simmer with low heat till the sauce become thick.
