The ingredients needed to make Bitter Gourd And Tofu Pork Soup:
  1. Make ready 300 grams ground pork
  2. Make ready 1 bitter gourd (thinly slice)
  3. Prepare 1 pack soft tofu
  4. Prepare 2 garlic and 4 small onion (pounded)
  5. Make ready 2 inches pounded ginger
  6. Make ready 10 small shallot (thinly slice)
  7. Make ready 1 tbsp light soy sauce
  8. Make ready Season with salt and pepper
  9. Take Optional add on small Chili pepper / spring onion
Steps to make Bitter Gourd And Tofu Pork Soup:
  1. With oil deep fry the shallot till its lightly brown but not burn then drain and set aside as garnish
  2. Pan fried garlic, ginger and onion with the minces pork(season with 2 tsp of salt) for 1 minutes then add in the water and bring it to a boil
  3. Add in the slice bitter gourd and tofu and let it simmer for 10 minutes then season it and off heat (optional add on small Chili pepper)
  4. To serve it as a soup simply garnish with a drizzle a little of the shallot oil and TOP with some crispy shallot and spring onion and small Chili pepper
