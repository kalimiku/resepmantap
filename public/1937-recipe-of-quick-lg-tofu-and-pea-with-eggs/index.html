The ingredients needed to make LG TOFU AND PEA WITH EGGS:
  1. Make ready 1 1/2 tbsp oil
  2. Take 1 tbsp each of fine dice garlic and shallot
  3. Take 1 packages soft tofu
  4. Take 1 cup sweet pea
  5. Prepare 1 cup any stock
  6. Get 2 red chili pepper
  7. Get 1 cup spring onion
  8. Make ready 2 eggs
  9. Make ready 1 dash salt and pepper

Instructions to make LG TOFU AND PEA WITH EGGS:
  1. In a shallow pan with oil sauté dice garlic and shallot then add tofu and sweet pea with red chili pepper
  2. Add stock and spring onion bring it to a boil
  3. Break 2 eggs in and swirl gently to break the egg yolk then season with salt and pepper, once stock start to comes to a boil off heat and cover lid till egg white are set
