The ingredients needed to make Green Tahini Sauce #vegan #gluten-free #paleo #5minuterecipe:
  1. Take 2 table spoon tahini
  2. Take 1 lemon, juice only
  3. Get 1 tablespoon olive oil (optional - can replace with water)
  4. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
  5. Take 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  6. Make ready 1-2 fresh garlic cloves (more to taste)
  7. Prepare 50 g fresh parsley, washed (leaves and stalks, or stalks only is fine)
  8. Make ready 2 tables spoons of water (more for a more liquid consistency)
Instructions to make Green Tahini Sauce #vegan #gluten-free #paleo #5minuterecipe:
  1. Combine all ingredients in a food processor and whirl for about 30 seconds until the mixture is smooth. Add more water if required.
  2. Serve! Here pictured with roasted cauliflower and salmon and with sweet potato.
  3. Any left overs can keep in the fridge for days - I usually keep it in a glass container and use throughout the week.
