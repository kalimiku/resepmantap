The ingredients needed to make Vegan strawberry and raspberry overnight oats:
  1. Make ready 6 tbsp vegan yogurt
  2. Prepare 25 g oats
  3. Prepare 4 large strawberries
  4. Make ready 2 tbsp raspberries
  5. Get 1 tsp honey
  6. Prepare 1 tsp chia seeds
