The ingredients needed to make Vegan cherry bakewell overnight oats:
  1. Prepare Handful pitted or sliced cherries
  2. Take 25 g oats
  3. Prepare 6 tbsp Vegan yogurt
  4. Make ready 2 tbsp ground almonds
  5. Take Whole almonds for topping
  6. Get 1 tbsp honey or dandelion honey

Steps to make Vegan cherry bakewell overnight oats:
  1. Start with 2 tbsp yogurt then add a layer of fruit and then a layer of ground almonds
  2. Continue to layer, finishing with yogurt. Add fruit and drizzle with honey.
  3. Pop on a lid and into the refrigerator overnight. Enjoy as a healthy and quick breakfast.
