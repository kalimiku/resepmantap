The ingredients needed to make Greek burgers with potatoes:
  1. Make ready Potatoes
  2. Get 750 g potatoes
  3. Take 1 lemon juice
  4. Take 2 tbsp Mustard
  5. Prepare Origano
  6. Take Black pepper
  7. Take 6 tbsp Olive oil
  8. Get Burgers
  9. Make ready 600 g 15% fat mince
  10. Take 1 slice bread
  11. Make ready 10 cherry tomatos
  12. Take 1 tbsp olive oil
  13. Get 1 tbsp vinegar
Instructions to make Greek burgers with potatoes:
  1. Peel the potatoes and cut into 2cm cubes. If using new potatoes no need to peel, just quarter. Put in oven proof dish.
  2. Season potatoes with salt, pepper and oregano
  3. Mix the lemon, mustard, oil and water in a bowl. Add to potatoes and mix. The potatoes should be 2/3 submerged. If not then add more water
  4. Cover with foil and cook for 1 hour at 200oC. While cooking make the burgers. After an hour, remove foil, stir and return to oven for 20min
  5. Finely chop the onions, tomatoes and make breadcrumbs out of the bread. Mix together by hand with the mince
  6. Form the mince into 6 equal sized burgers. Place on top of the potatoes and cook for 30min or until the burgers have browned
  7. Turn over the burgers and cook for another 25min
  8. Serve with feta cheese
