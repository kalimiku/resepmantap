The ingredients needed to make Sweet Potato Waffles:
  1. Get 1 cup flour
  2. Get 1 cup almond milk
  3. Take 2 spoons sugar
  4. Make ready 1 sweet potato (pre-cooked in the oven)
  5. Get 1 tsp salt
  6. Prepare 2 tsp baking soda
  7. Prepare 1 tsp nutmeg
  8. Get 1 egg
  9. Make ready 2 spoons butter

Instructions to make Sweet Potato Waffles:
  1. In one bowl mix the flower, sugar, baking soda, salt and nutmeg.
  2. In another bowl mix the milk, melted butter and 1 egg yolk. Then add the sweet potato and mix gently
  3. Make a whole in the flour and add the wet ingredients
  4. Mix the egg white into fluffly
  5. Add the egg white into the mix and mix gently (leave some white pieces intact)
  6. Put the mix into the waffle machine for 5 min
  7. Done! Add the toppings you want
