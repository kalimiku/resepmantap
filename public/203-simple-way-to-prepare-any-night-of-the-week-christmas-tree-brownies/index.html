The ingredients needed to make Christmas tree brownies:
  1. Take For brownie:
  2. Make ready 100 gms Maida
  3. Take 3 tbsp Butter
  4. Make ready 1/2 tsp Baking powder
  5. Make ready 1/4 tsp Soda
  6. Make ready 200 gms Milkmaid
  7. Make ready 2 tbsp Cocoa powder
  8. Make ready 2 tbsp Milk powder
  9. Make ready 1 tsp Vanilla essence
  10. Take 50 ml Water
  11. Take Non dairy fresh cream whipped
  12. Get 3-4 drops Green colour
  13. Get Gems
  14. Take Sprinklers

Steps to make Christmas tree brownies:
  1. Mix all ingredients of brownie and bake @ 180 ' for 15- 20 mins,let cool
  2. Cut triangle with the help of cutter
  3. Insert a straw in the middle of the brownie,
  4. Decorate with cream, gems and sprinklers
