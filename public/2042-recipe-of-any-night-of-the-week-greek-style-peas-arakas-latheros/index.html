  2. Take 1 onion finely chopped
  3. Prepare 2 carrots cut into chunks
  4. Get 2 potatoes cut into chunks
  5. Make ready 1 tin good quality tinned tomatoes
  6. Take 4 tablespoons Good quality olive oil
  7. Make ready Parsley & Greek feta for decoration
