The ingredients needed to make Chicken & butter bean bake:
  1. Get 4-6 chicken thighs
  2. Take 200 g tin butter beans
  3. Make ready 1 carrot
  4. Prepare 1 small onion or 1 leek
  5. Take 2 sticks celery
  6. Get Half red pepper
  7. Prepare 300 ml chicken stock
  8. Make ready Thyme or mixed herbs
  9. Get Spinach (fresh or frozen)

Instructions to make Chicken & butter bean bake:
  1. Turn the oven to 180 degrees
  2. Chop the veg into small pieces and fry for 5 minutes
  3. Place veg in bottom of roasting dish
  4. Fry the chicken until nicely coloured and add to the roasting dish
  5. Add the butter beans, spinach, stock and herbs. Grind some black pepper over the chicken
  6. Place in the oven for 45 - 60 minutes
  7. Serve with rice
