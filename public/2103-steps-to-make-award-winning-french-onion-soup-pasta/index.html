The ingredients needed to make French onion soup pasta:
  1. Prepare 250 g orecchitte pasta (or other pasta)
  2. Make ready 500 g white onions
  3. Prepare 660 ml stock
  4. Make ready 2 tbsp oil
  5. Make ready 60 ml port
  6. Make ready 60 g cheddar/gruyere/vegan cheese grated (optional)
  7. Take to taste Salt and pepper
  8. Make ready Handful chopped parsley (optional)
  9. Get 120 ml water

Sweet onions are caramelized in olive oil and butter to form the base of the sauce. All the comforting flavours of French onion soup in a pasta bake, complete with cheesy crouton topping. Melt the butter and oil in a large non-stick saucepan. Stir in the onions with a generous pinch of salt.

Instructions to make French onion soup pasta:
  1. Peel the onions and slice them as thin as possible using a food processor (slicing adaptor) or a mandolin. You can also just do this with a sharp knife!
  2. Put the oil in a large lidded pot with the onions and sautee for 20-25min until the onions are brown and caramelized. Keep the lid on but stir the mixture every 5 minutes or so. Keep an eye on the onions and when they stick to the bottom of the pot add a splash of water and stir.
  3. When the onions are brown and really soft, add the pasta, stock, water and port. Stir everything together and cook with the lid on till the pasta is cooked and most of the liquid is absorbed, about 15 minutes.
  4. Turn off the heat and stir in the grated cheese if using. Taste and season with salt and pepper as desired. Sprinkle chopped parsley if using. Serve in a bowl and enjoy!
