The ingredients needed to make Shiitake Mushroom and Beef Fry:
  1. Prepare 200 g minced beef
  2. Get 7 shiitake mushroom
  3. Take 1/2 onion
  4. Prepare 2 teaspoon soy sauce
  5. Take 1 Cube Maggie
  6. Prepare Pepper
  7. Make ready egg
  8. Prepare flour
  9. Make ready Pablo bread crumbs
Steps to make Shiitake Mushroom and Beef Fry:
  1. Cut stalks and chop small. Chop onion.
  2. Mix all ingredients and season with Maggie cube, soy sauce and pepper, salt to taste.
  3. Flour mushroom and put beef mixture on it.
  4. Put flour, egg and panko bread crumbs.
  5. Fry in vegetable oil for 5 minutes.
  6. Garnish with shredded cabbage.
