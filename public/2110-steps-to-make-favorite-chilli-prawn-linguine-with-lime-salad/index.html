The ingredients needed to make Chilli prawn linguine (with lime salad):
  1. Make ready 170 g linguine pasta
  2. Prepare 100 g sugar snap peas
  3. Take 1 tbsp olive oil
  4. Make ready 1 garlic clove
  5. Get 1 large red chilli
  6. Take 200 g raw king prawns
  7. Make ready 250 g mini plum tomatoes
  8. Make ready Fresh basil
  9. Prepare Side Salad
  10. Prepare leaves Salad
  11. Prepare 1 tbsp Greek yoghurt
  12. Make ready 1 lime
  13. Prepare 1 tsp golden caster sugar
  14. Take Salt & pepper

Instructions to make Chilli prawn linguine (with lime salad):
  1. SALAD DRESSING: - Mix Greek yoghurt with the juice of 1 lime and caster sugar. Season with salt & pepper. Pour over salad leaves and place to one side.
  2. Place linguine into a large saucepan and cover with boiling water. Set a timer for 10 mins.
  3. Meanwhile heat the olive oil in a large frying pan. Add finely chopped garlic and deseeded finely chopped chilli. Fry gently for 30 secs (don’t let the garlic brown).
  4. Add raw king prawns to the frying pan and cook over a high heat for 3 mins until pink.
  5. Add sliced tomatoes to the king prawns and cook for a further 3 mins until softened.
  6. When the timer for the pasta goes off add sugar snap peas to the pasta and cook for a further two mins.
  7. Drain the pasta & sugar snaps and mix into the frying pan.
  8. Serve with fresh basil, a grating of Parmesan and the side salad
