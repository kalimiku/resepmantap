  2. Get 200 g cooked lentils
  3. Get 1 carrot
  4. Make ready 1 stick celery
  5. Take 1 small onion
  6. Make ready Half red pepper
  7. Prepare 2 tablespoons tomato purée
  8. Get 1 chicken / veg stock cube
  9. Take 1 teaspoon mustard

Instructions to make Sausage and lentil casserole (GF):
  1. Heat the oven to 190 degrees C
  2. Place the sausages in the oven for 20 minutes
  3. Chop the carrot, onion, celery and pepper. Fry for 5-6 minutes until soft
  4. Add tomato purée and the stock cube in 200ml water
  5. Add the lentils and mustard and warm through for a few minutes
  6. Once cooked, add the sausages and serve with vegetables
