The ingredients needed to make Dill & Spring Onion Pancakes:
  1. Get 100 g plain flour,
  2. Prepare 100 g fat free quark,
  3. Make ready 2 medium eggs,
  4. Get 2 finely chopped spring onions,
  5. Get 2 level tsp dried dill,
  6. Get 1 tsp baking powder,
  7. Make ready 1 good pinch of salt,
  8. Make ready 1 pinch white pepper,
  9. Take Spray oil such as Frylight for frying

Steps to make Dill & Spring Onion Pancakes:
  1. In a bowl add the flour, salt, white pepper and baking powder. Mix together.
  2. Add the quark and the egg and fold together then mix really well until a lovely thick smooth batter is formed.
  3. Add the dill and spring onions. Mix together until evenly distributed throughout the batter. Preheat your non stick frying pan over a medium high heat and spray in some oil. Once hot dollop in a spoon of batter and flatten it out into a neat circle shape around 1cm thick.
  4. Fry for a couple of minutes then flip over until golden on both sides. Remove and set aside then begin the next pancake. Continue until you've cooked them all, eat whilst still warm and serve with your chosen sides and enjoy! :)
