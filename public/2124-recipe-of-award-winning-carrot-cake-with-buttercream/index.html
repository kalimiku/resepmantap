The ingredients needed to make Carrot Cake with buttercream:
  1. Prepare 6 eggs
  2. Prepare 420 gr flour
  3. Make ready 450 ml vegetable oil
  4. Prepare 2,5 tsp cinnamon powder
  5. Get 400 gr sugar
  6. Get 350 gr grated carrots
  7. Get 2 tsp baking powder
  8. Take 1/2 tsp salt
  9. Take For buttercream
  10. Take 250 gr butter (room temperature)
  11. Take 500 gr icing sugar
  12. Get 1 tsp vanilla extract
  13. Prepare 1 tbsp milk

Instructions to make Carrot Cake with buttercream:
  1. Mix eggs and sugar with mixer for 10 mins add oil and vanilla extract keep mixing. Add carrots and stir them
  2. In another bowl put flour, baking powder and cinnamon. Mix them well. Add them to liquid mix and stir them with a spatula gently.
  3. Preheat your oven 180°c and use two 10 inc circle baking tins. Share your cake mix with two tins. Bake them nearly for 40 mins.
  4. For buttercream:
  5. Put your buttercream in a deep bowl and mix it well for 5 mins add icing sugar little by little and keep mixing for 10 mins add vanilla extract and milk mix for 2 more mins.
  6. Spread your buttercream between cakes and spread on top as well. Put walnuts on your cake. Enjoy your cake!! 🥰
