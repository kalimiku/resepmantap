Instructions to make Jel Christmas Cake:
  1. Preheat the oven to 180 degree c on conventional mode
  2. Take one bowl take the condensed milk and add butter mix it
  3. In another bowl to sieve maida baking soda and baking powder
  4. Now this thing add in methai made and butter.
  5. Mix it gently add in vanilla essence.
  6. Add milk or water to adjust consistency
  7. Pour it in the greased cake tin.
  8. Bake cake in convection mode for 35 to 40 minutes
  9. Cool cake and cut it horizontally into layers
  10. Now take fresh cream whippd it with the help of better.
  11. When you see cream is stiff so cream is ready for icing
  12. Put it in fridge for 15 minutes
  13. Apply this sugar syrup on cake layer.
  14. Take one layer of cake apply whipped cream place the another layer and cover it with cream on the top side.
  15. Now stick sugar print photo on center of cake.
  16. Method For Gel :-
  17. Dissolve gelatin in the water for 2 minutes.
  18. Place in the microwave for 20 to 30 seconds.
  19. In another pan add corn syrup heat it on on low flame.
  20. When corn syrup is hot add this on gelatin mixture.
  21. Mix it well.
  22. Your piping gel is ready.
  23. Take two bowl. Add gel and red colour. In another bowl add green colour and gel. Mix it well.
  24. Now pour in both colour gel in piping begs.
  25. Make design on cake with the help of piping gel beg or spoon.
  26. Your jel cake is ready.
