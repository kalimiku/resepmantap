Instructions to make Vegetarian lentil curry:
  1. Rinse the lentils and soak in plenty of water for at least 4 hours.
  2. Chop onion and garlic and fry in a bit of oil until they're soft. Add curry powder and cook for another minute.
  3. Add the rest of the ingredients and simmer for about about 30 minutes (check the potatoes and lentils are done).
  4. Enjoy!
