The ingredients needed to make Vegan Strawberry brownies:
  1. Take 3 tablespoons cocoa powder
  2. Prepare 150 grams chocolate for melting
  3. Make ready 250 ml vegan milk (I chose soy milk)
  4. Get 1 cup strawberries
  5. Get 170 grams flour
  6. Get 4 tablespoons sugar
  7. Make ready 1 tablespoon baking powder
  8. Make ready 1 pinch salt
  9. Get 5 tablespoons oil
  10. Make ready 1 cup nuts of your choice (optional)
  11. Make ready Vegan butter to grease the pan

Steps to make Vegan Strawberry brownies:
  1. Preheat your oven on 180 degrees celsius and grease a pan of your choice. Chop the strawberries into larger chunks.
  2. Mix together the dry ingredients: the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder and the salt.
  3. Add the milk and the oil to the dry ingredients and mix well to incorporate everything.
  4. Melt the chocolate in a pan over the stove and then add it to the mixture.
  5. Chop the nuts but not too fine, to leave some texture and add them to the mixture. Then add the mixture to your greased pan.
  6. Add the strawberries on top. You can get creative and do some models. I just plopped them in there.
  7. Bake your brownies in the oven for about 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean. I was lucky with the transparent pan, as I could see the sponge forming. Highly recommend!
