The ingredients needed to make Pork fried rice:
  1. Get 50 g butter
  2. Make ready 100 g pork oil
  3. Get 100g pork loin (dice sliced)
  4. Get 3 beef sausage
  5. Take Green leek union
  6. Prepare 10 pcs garlic
  7. Get 7 pcs shallots
  8. Make ready 7 pcs red chili
  9. Prepare Salt
  10. Get Sugar
