Steps to make Grape fruit juice:
  1. Take grape fruits and wash properly
  2. Peel outer skin and separate all the slices.
  3. Remove white covering from each slice and remove seeds. Separate pulp. (removing white covering is necessary to avoid bitterness. Otherwise Other easy way is to cut the fruit in two parts and squeeze juice directly but there is a chance that it will be bitter)
  4. Collect pulp in blender, add water, salt and pepper and blend
  5. Pour into glasses and serve. I used with pulp. (If you want you can strain the pulp and than use if you like)
