The ingredients needed to make My Peking Chicken + Pork Dish ü•∞:
  1. Take 1 large chicken breasts cut cut into 1/4 inch thick
  2. Make ready 1/2 length of a Tenderloin pork sliced 1/4 Inch thick
  3. Take Oil for Deep frying
  4. Make ready 1 tsp Toasted sesame seeds
  5. Take MARINADE……..
  6. Prepare 1 Egg
  7. Make ready 1 tbls Cornstarch/flour
  8. Make ready 1/2 tsp Salt
  9. Take SAUCE………
  10. Get 1 +1/2 tbls Tomato ketchup
  11. Make ready 1/2 tbls Chilli sauce
  12. Get 1/4 tsp Sweetbean sauce or Hoisin
  13. Make ready 1 tbls Worsestershire sauce
  14. Prepare 1 +1/2 tbls Chinese Black vinegar or Rice Wine also Rice Vinegar
  15. Get 1 +1/2 tbls Sugar (I use half that amount)
  16. Take 1 Pinch Chinese 5 spice powder (optional)
  17. Take 2 tbls Water
Steps to make My Peking Chicken + Pork Dish ü•∞:
  1. Pound chicken breasts slices with a mallet until tender. Then slice the pork. Set aside. In a bowl combine the marinade ingredients add in chicken slices mix and cover and marinate for 30 minutes.
  3. In a separate bowl mix the sauce ingredients you can use less sugar and adapt it to your taste by adding a little more or less of the other sauce ingredients.
  5. Heat up the wok with enough oil to cover the chicken slices when deep frying. Fry for 5 minutes until chicken is coloured and a little crisp. Take out the chicken drain on kitchen roll and set aside.
  7. In a Saucepan Bring the sauce to a quick boil add it to deep fried chicken stir until all the chicken is covered in the sauce. Then in a dry hot pan toast the sesame seeds moving them around for about 2 minutes set aside
  8. Add the chicken and sauce to a serving dish and sprinkle all on top the toasted sesame seeds. Serve over a bowl of hot steamed Rice.
