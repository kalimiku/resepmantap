Instructions to make Yeast Pizza Dough (vegan pizzas) üå±:
  1. Put the flour in to the bowl and make a well in the centre. Add sugar, the yeast and a little warm water. It will bubble a little. Continue to add water until you have a soft ball of dough.
  2. Add the oil and knead on a floured surface for at least 10 minutes until the dough is elastic. Cover with a clean tea towel and put in a warm place to rise for 2 hours. It will double in size.
