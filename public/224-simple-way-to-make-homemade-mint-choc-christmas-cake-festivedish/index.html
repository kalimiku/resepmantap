The ingredients needed to make Mint-choc Christmas cake #festivedish:
  1. Take 200 g margarine/butter
  2. Take 1 cup sugar (reduce or add as preferred)
  3. Take 2 cups Self rising flour
  4. Get 1 teaspoon Mint essence
  5. Take 80 g Mint chocolate (Cadbury‚Äôs packet)
  6. Make ready 2 eggs
  7. Make ready 5 tablespoons cocoa powder
  8. Get 2/3 cups milk
  9. Take 1 cup strong black coffee
  10. Make ready 1 tablespoon vanilla essence
  11. Prepare For the icing glaze
  12. Prepare 2 cups icing sugar
  13. Prepare 1/2 cup lemon juice/ orange juice
  14. Prepare Zest of lemon/ orange
  15. Get Decorations
  16. Get 2 packets skittles
  17. Take 1 packet Oreo biscuits
Instructions to make Mint-choc Christmas cake #festivedish:
  1. Pre-heat oven at 180 degrees. Prepare baking tin or sufuria
  2. First melt the chocolate in a microwave until smooth
  3. In a large bowl, beat the margarine/butter with sugar until pale and well mixed
  4. Add the eggs slowly, one at a time, as you continue beating the butter- sugar mixture
  5. Add half of the melted chocolate into the mixture and gently mix using a mwiko preferably (keep the other half for the wet ingredients)
  6. Sift the flour and cocoa powder powder together. Then alternatively add in the flour and the milk to the previous mixture starting with the flour and ending with the flour. Mix gently with every addition.
  7. Add in the coffee (coffee is added to enhance the chocolate flavor) gently and steadily, add the other half of the melted chocolate, the mint essence and vanilla essence. Be careful not to over beat the cake batter.
  8. Put the cake batter into your prepared baking tin or sufuria and bake for 45 minutes or until toothpick or knife comes out clear. Let it cool completely.

  10. After cake cooling, pour the icing glaze in preferred fashion over the cake and decorate with the skittles as desired.
