The ingredients needed to make Pork ribs soup with white gourd 清爽冬瓜排骨汤:
  1. Prepare 500 g pork ribs
  2. Prepare 300 g white gourd
  3. Get 5 ginger slices
  4. Prepare 1 spring onion
  5. Make ready 1 spoon salt

Instructions to make Pork ribs soup with white gourd 清爽冬瓜排骨汤:
  1. Add pork ribs and ginger slices to pot, add 1L water. Boil with medium heat. Make sure to skim off the scum.
  2. Cut white gourd into medium size cubes and add to the pot.
  3. Cook for 20min in pressure cooker or 1 hour in normal cooker.
  4. Finally add salt and chopped spring onions.
