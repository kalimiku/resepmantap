The ingredients needed to make Pork mince pasta:
  1. Make ready 500 g pork mince
  2. Make ready 300 g pasta shells
  3. Make ready 1 carton pasata
  4. Make ready Garlic paste
  5. Take 1 red onion
  6. Make ready 2 stalks celery
  7. Prepare 1 large carrot
  8. Make ready Sage
  9. Get Salt and black pepper
  10. Prepare 1 tablespoon oil
  11. Take Pork gravy powder
  12. Prepare 4 handfuls grated cheddar cheese

Steps to make Pork mince pasta:
  1. Dice the onion, celery and carrot. Add the oil to the saucepan and gently start to soften the vegetables. Add a pinch of salt and a 2 tsp size squeeze of garlic paste. Stir for 5 mins.
  2. Fill the kettle and boil it.
  3. Add the mince sage and a little dash of gravy powder to the pan and continue stirring. Break up and large lumps that the meat forms as you stir.
  4. Add the pasata and bring to the boil. Add the boiling water to another pan along with a pinch of salt to cook the pasta shells. Cook the shells according to packet instructions, approx 15 mins.
  5. Drain the pasta, and serve in bowls. Spoon over a generous amount of the meat sauce and top with a handful of cheese to melt in.
