The ingredients needed to make Parma ham, egg & capers open sandwich:
  1. Take 2 hard-boiled eggs, sliced
  2. Prepare 2 slices sourdough bread (I used sourdough spelt bread but any sourdough bread works)
  3. Prepare Good-quality butter (I used salted Guernsey butter on this occasion)
  4. Make ready 6 slices Parma ham
  5. Prepare 2 large lettuce leaves
  6. Take 1 large vine tomato, sliced
  7. Take Small pinch sea salt
  8. Prepare 8 thin slices cucumber
  9. Get 2 tsp capers
  10. Prepare 1 tbsp mayonnaise
  11. Get 1/4 tsp lemon juice
  12. Make ready A few crisps

Steps to make Parma ham, egg & capers open sandwich:
  1. Butter one (now top!) side of each bread slice, generously but not over-profusely.
  2. To each slice of bread, add a slice of Parma ham and smear a little butter over it.
  3. Next add another slice of Parma ham, then slices of hard-boiled egg and then a final slice of Parma ham.
  4. Add a lettuce leaf to each slice of bread, then some tomato slices. Sprinkle a very little sea salt on the tomatoes and then add 4 slices of cucumber.
  5. Mix the capers, mayonnaise and lemon juice together and carefully spoon over the salad items.
  6. Garnish with a few crisps.
