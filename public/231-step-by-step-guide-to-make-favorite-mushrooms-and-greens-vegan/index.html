  1. Prepare 1 tbsp coconut oil

  5. Get 250 g mushrooms, chopped
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp white miso paste + some hot water
  7. Prepare Couple of handfuls of greens eg komatsuna or baby spinach
  8. Make ready To garnish: sesame seeds, mirin, chopped spring onion
