The ingredients needed to make Cacio and pepe breadsticks with prosciutto and goat's cheese:
  1. Prepare 100 g flour
  2. Get 50 g cold butter
  3. Make ready 35 ml cold milk
  4. Prepare 20 g Pecorino cheese
  5. Take Lots of black pepper
  6. Prepare Egg to glaze
  7. Get Prosciutto and cheese to serve

Instructions to make Cacio and pepe breadsticks with prosciutto and goat's cheese:
  1. Rub butter and flour together until you get breadcrumbs. Add cheese and pepper.
  2. Add milk gradually, bring it together with your fingers until you get a dough. Wrap in cling film and leave rest in the fridge for an hour. Preheat oven to 180. Divide dough into even sections
  3. Roll out with your hands into sticks. Glaze with egg, sprinkle with pepper and bake for about 18-20 mins. Leave to cool on a wire rack. When ready, start to create a little structure as shown, like a weaved basket
