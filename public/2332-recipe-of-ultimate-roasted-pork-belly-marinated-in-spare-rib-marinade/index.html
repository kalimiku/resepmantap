  2. Get *Note: Skin can be baked separately for Pork Crackling Chips
  3. Prepare Marinade

  8. Take 1 tablespoon Tomato Paste

Instructions to make Roasted Pork Belly Marinated in Spare Rib Marinade:
  1. Combine all the marinade ingredients in a baking dish or bowl.
  2. Cut Pork Belly Block in half, make holes by sticking some skewers or a fork several times. Add to the marinade, coat well, and marinate for overnight or half a day.
  3. Place the Pork in a clean baking dish and place marinade into a small saucepan.
  4. Cover the baking dish with a sheet of foil, and bake in the pre-heated oven at 180℃ for 30 minutes (OR 40 minutes if thick). Remove the foil, raise the heat to 200℃, and bake for 15 minutes or until nicely browned. Rest for 10 minutes before serve.
  5. Cook the marinade in the saucepan until slightly thick and serve it as a sauce.
