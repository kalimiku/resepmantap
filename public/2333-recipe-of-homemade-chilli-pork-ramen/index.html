The ingredients needed to make Chilli pork ramen:
  1. Take Broth
  2. Take 2 litre Chicken broth (use good quality stock if not making own broth)
  3. Take 1 tspn Mirin
  4. Prepare Ginger (thumb size piece)
  5. Take 2 large cloves of garlic
  6. Make ready Handful pea shoots
  7. Take 1 small red onion diced thin
  8. Get 1 red pepper thinly sliced
  9. Get 1 large carrot thinly sliced
  10. Get 1 spring onion thinly sliced
  11. Take Splash soy sauce
  12. Prepare 1 lime wedge for garnish
  13. Make ready Sprinkle of fresh coriander for garnish
  14. Prepare 1 large red chilli sliced thin
  15. Make ready 4 nests of noodles
  16. Take Pork
  17. Get 4 lean pork loins
  18. Prepare Barbecue sauce

Instructions to make Chilli pork ramen:
  1. Marinate your pork steaks in barbecue sauce for at least an hour. I use a Tupperware and leave them in the fridge sealed. Once marinated and ready, griddle the pork for a few minutes either side so you have nice lines on them. Leave aside for a few mins to rest.
  2. Meanwhile, prepare all your veg. I like to slice everything long and thin so they can be eaten easily with the noodles.
  3. Get your chicken broth bubbling and add your ginger and garlic. Cook your noodles in the broth and then add all your veggies except your pea shoots (save a few bits of red onion to sprinkle on top for garnish). Thinly slice your pork. Layer your broth and noodles into a bowl and add the pork on top. Add the pea shoots to the bowl next to the pork and red onion, chilli and coriander. Garnish with a lime wedge too. Add soy sauce if required. Enjoy!
