The ingredients needed to make My burrito beef rice:
  1. Make ready 3 cups basmatti rice
  2. Get 3 cups cold water
  3. Prepare 500 g minced(ground) beef
  4. Prepare 2 tbsp oil
  5. Make ready 1 large onion finely diced
  6. Prepare 1 orange pepper finely diced
  7. Get 1 Serrano chilli finely diced
  8. Prepare 3 tbsp ground cumin
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp cayenne pepper
  10. Prepare 1 tbsp onion powder
  11. Take 1 tbsp smoked paprika
  12. Prepare 1 tbsp garlic powder
  13. Make ready 1 tbsp dried oregano
  14. Prepare 1 beef stock cube
  15. Get 300 ml water
  16. Make ready to taste Salt and pepper

Steps to make My burrito beef rice:
  1. In a pot put rice and water equal amounts on to boil. Once boiled turn to a simmer for 10 mins then turn off leave to stream.
  2. Add oil into a large pan/wok and stir fry lean mince beef
  3. Add chopped veg and spices and stir fry for 5 mins
  4. Add stock cube and water bring to a boil then reduce heat to simmer
  5. Once liquid has reduced add fluffed up rice and mix well
  6. Eat as is or on a wrap with any toppings u like….enjoy
