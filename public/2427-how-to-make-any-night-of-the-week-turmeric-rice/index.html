The ingredients needed to make Turmeric rice:
  1. Make ready 1 1/2 cup basmati rice
  2. Get 1 medium onion
  3. Get 1 tsp pilau masala
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp turmeric powder
  5. Prepare salt and pepper
  6. Take 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  7. Take 3 tbsp ghee
  8. Prepare 2 1/2 cups chicken stock

Instructions to make Turmeric rice:
  1. Melt ghee in a sufuria and add the onions and the ginger garlic paste. let cook till onions are tender. Add in the turmeric, pilau masala, salt and pepper
  2. Add in the rice and give a good mix, until well coated
  3. Add in the stock and stir.
  4. Let simmer till all liquid is absorbed
  5. Serve with your favorite stew and don't forget to enjoy!
