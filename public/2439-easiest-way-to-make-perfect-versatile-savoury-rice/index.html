The ingredients needed to make Versatile Savoury Rice:
  1. Make ready 250 g basmati rice
  2. Take 2 tbsp ground nut oil
  3. Prepare 1 medium onion
  4. Prepare carrots, courgettes, mushrooms, bell peppers, chilli peppers
  5. Get Tamari soy sauce
  6. Prepare Chicken stock
  7. Make ready Mixed dry herbs
  8. Prepare to taste Salt & pepper
Steps to make Versatile Savoury Rice:
  1. Boil the rice for 10 mins and then rinse under cold running water until water runs clear.
  2. In a high sided pan, heat the oil and then add the onions. Cook for 5 mins until softened and then add the other vegetables if desired.
  3. Cook vegetables for another 3 mins and then add the rice, mixed herbs, salt and pepper and "fry" for a further 10 mins.
  4. Add several glugs of soy sauce to the pan and coat the rice. Then add the mixed herbs and the chicken stock, about a cup and cover. Keep adding the chicken stock a little at a time until cooked the rice is cooked
  5. Season with salt and pepper and then serve as a main dish or a side. 8
