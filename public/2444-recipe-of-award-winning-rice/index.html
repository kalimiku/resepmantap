Instructions to make Rice:
  1. Wash the rice and soak in water for 30 minutes
  2. Drain the water
  3. Heat oil in a pan.
  4. When the oil is very hot, add chopped onion and fry for a minute.
  5. Then add cumin seeds and fry for a minute
  6. Add two cups of water, salt and black peper
  7. When it comes to boils add soaked rice and mix it.
  8. Cover the pan and cook the rice on low heat till its done and all the water is absorbed.switch off the heat and rest it for 15 minutes.
  9. Serve hot
