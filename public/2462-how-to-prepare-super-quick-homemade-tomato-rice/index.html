The ingredients needed to make Tomato Rice:
  1. Get 1.5 cup Basmati rice
  2. Make ready 3-4 tomatoes thinly sliced
  3. Prepare to taste Salt
  4. Prepare 1 tsp Green chilli Ginger Paste
  5. Take As required Oil
  6. Take 1 Cinnamon
  7. Make ready 1 tsp jeera/ cumin seeds
  8. Prepare 2 Dry red chillies
  9. Make ready 1 Onion cut in cubes
  10. Take Handful mint leaves
  11. Prepare Handful Coriander leaves
  12. Make ready 1 tsp Chana dal
  13. Make ready 1 tbsp red chilli powder
  14. Take 1 tsp turmeric powder
Steps to make Tomato Rice:
  1. Take oil in Cooker add jeera, chana dal, dry red chillies, cinnamon roast it for a while add onions, green chillies and ginger paste and cook for a while.
  2. Now add all masalas, add tomatoes and let it cook till it turns soft. Add soaked rice and add 3 cups water and cook for 1 whistle. Serve with boondi raita and Papad.
  3. Tip. Water proportion should be precise 1.5 cup rice requires 3 cups water.Otherwise it will turn soggy
