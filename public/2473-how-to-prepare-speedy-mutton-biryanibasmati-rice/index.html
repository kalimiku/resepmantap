The ingredients needed to make MUTTON BIRYANI(BASMATI RICE):
  1. Make ready 3 cups Basmati Rice (Green Pack)
  2. Get 750 gms Mutton
  3. Take 4 Onions, big sized
  4. Get 6 Tomatoes, Medium sized
  5. Take 3 tbsp Ginger Garlic paste
  6. Get 6 Green chillies
  7. Take 1/2 cup Curd
  8. Take 1 Cinnamon
  9. Make ready 2 Cloves
  10. Make ready 3 Bay leaves
  11. Prepare 4 Cardamoms
  12. Take 3 Chilli powder (adjust to your taste)
  13. Prepare 1/4 tbsp Turmeric powder -
  14. Get Handful coriander and mint leaves
  15. Make ready 3 tbsp Ghee
  16. Make ready 1/2 cup Oil
  17. Take As per taste Salt
  18. Take 4.5 cups Water
