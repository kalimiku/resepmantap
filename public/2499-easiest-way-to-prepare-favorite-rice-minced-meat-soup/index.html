The ingredients needed to make Rice_Minced_Meat_Soup:
  1. Get 1 cup egyptian rice
  2. Prepare 100 g minced meat - beef or lamb as desired
  3. Get 1 tbsp ghee
  4. Take 1/2 tsp seven spice
  5. Prepare 5 cups water
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp parsley - chopped

Instructions to make Rice_Minced_Meat_Soup:
  1. Wash the rice and strain well, leave aside.
  2. In a deep pot, add the ghee and stir in the minced meat saute until browned and cooked, add the rice, seven spices and salt. Add in the water and stir for a few minutes.
  3. Cover the pot and simmer on low heat for 30- 45 minutes or until the rice is cooked & the soup thickens.
  4. Finally transfer the soup to your choice of serving dish and garnish with the chopped parsley.
  5. Serve hot
