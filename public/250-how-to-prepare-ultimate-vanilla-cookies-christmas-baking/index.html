The ingredients needed to make Vanilla cookies. #Christmas baking#:
  1. Get 300 gms self raising flour
  2. Prepare 300 gms margarine
  3. Get 50 gms sugar. Adjust if need be
  4. Prepare 1 egg
  5. Take 1 tsp vanilla essence

Steps to make Vanilla cookies. #Christmas baking#:
  1. Grease n dust your baking tray.
  2. Assemble all your ingredients.
  3. In a mixing bowl, sieve your flour twice, Add sugar, margarine, an egg n vanilla essence. Note: you can do any other flavour of your choice. NOTE: The ratio of flour to Margarine is 1:1 for better results unless otherwise.
  4. Mix them all together using your clean hands.
  5. Make a good, soft dough.
  6. Transfer the dough to a clean floured surface n continue kneading shortly.
  7. Cover your dough with a cling film or clean cloth n let it rest for 15- 20 mins.
  8. While waiting for the dough to rest, pre heat your oven at 170 degrees.
  9. After 15 mins, roll out your dough to a not very thin, not very thick, then using your cookie cutter, cut out any shape that you want.
  10. Arrange them in the baking tray, make some designs on them using a toothpick. cover them again with a clean cloth for them to rest again for 10- 15 mins.
  11. After that bake them for 20-25 mins or till they are ready. should be golden brown.
  12. Enjoy with a drink of your choice.
