The ingredients needed to make Basmati rice payasam/rice kheer:
  1. Prepare 1/2 cup basmati rice (soaked)
  2. Make ready 3 cups full cream milk
  3. Make ready as required Cashew
  4. Take as required Crushed cardamom
  5. Take as required Ghee
  6. Get 1/4 cup sugar

Instructions to make Basmati rice payasam/rice kheer:
  1. In heavy-based pan boil the milk.
  2. Once the milk gets boiled add the rice into it, you have to cook the whole thing at low flame.
  3. Cook until the rice gets soft consistency,keep stirring so that you can avoid burning
  4. Once the rice gets soft add 1/4sugar you can also add jaggery instead
  5. Roast the chasew in ghee separately
  6. Once the sugar gets dissolve add the cardamom into it which gives flavor
  7. Finally add the ghee and cashew
  8. Serve it in a bowl and enjoy your dessert
