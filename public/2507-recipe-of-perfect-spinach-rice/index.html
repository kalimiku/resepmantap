The ingredients needed to make Spinach Rice:
  1. Get 1 cup basmati rice/rice
  2. Prepare 1 onion
  3. Take 1 cup palak(spinach) leaves
  4. Take 1 inch ginger
  5. Take 6-8 garlic cloves
  6. Get 1 green chilli
  7. Prepare 1 tsp garam masala
  8. Take 1 inch cinnemom stick
  9. Prepare 1 black Cardamom
  10. Make ready 3-4 peppercorns
  11. Get 2 cloves
  12. Make ready 1 star anise
  13. Take 1 bay leaf
  14. Get to taste Salt
  15. Get 1/2 tsp sugar
  16. Prepare as needed dry fruits (Cashews and raisins) for garnishing
  17. Prepare 1 tbsp ghee

Instructions to make Spinach Rice:
  1. In a pressure cooker add ghee roast cashews and raisins and keep aside.
  2. Grind palak, onion, chilli, ginger and garlic to smooth paste
  3. Add whole spices and saute. Add palak puree and saute till rawness goes
  4. Add washed and soaked rice and saute a minute
  5. Add garam masala, slat, sugar, garam masala and water and pressure cook for a whistle
  6. Garnish with cashews and raisins and serve
