The ingredients needed to make Rice Kheer:
  1. Prepare 1 Cup Rice(Basmati chawal)
  2. Take 1 &1/2 litre fresh Milk
  3. Take 1 Cup Sugar
  4. Take 4 Green Cardamom
  5. Get 12 Almonds chopped
  6. Prepare 1 Cup milk
  7. Get Few drops kewra water(Optional)

Steps to make Rice Kheer:
  1. 1 Cup rice soaked for 1/2 hour.
  2. In a blender add soaked rice, Cardamom seeds,1 cup milk and blend.
  3. In pot,add 1&1/2 litre milk and bring it to boil,add blended rice and cook for 8-10 minutes, keep stirring in between.
  4. Add sugar,mix well and cook on low flame for 10-15 minutes or until rice are cooked and keep stirring in between.
  5. Add chopped half almonds and,mix well.
