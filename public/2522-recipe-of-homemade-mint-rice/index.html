The ingredients needed to make Mint Rice:
  1. Make ready 2 cups basmati rice
  2. Make ready 1 bundle mint leaves
  3. Prepare 1/2 bundle coriander leaves
  4. Make ready 1/2 lemon juice
  5. Prepare 2-3 green chillies
  6. Make ready 2 tbsp oil
  7. Get 1 tbsp ghee
  8. Take 10 cashews
  9. Get 1 boiled potato chopped
  10. Make ready 1 small piece of cinnamon stick
  11. Take 1 tsp cumin seeds
  12. Prepare 3 finely chopped garlic cloves
  13. Prepare 1 onion finely chopped
