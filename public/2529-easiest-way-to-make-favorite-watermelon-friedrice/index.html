The ingredients needed to make Watermelon Friedrice:
  1. Make ready 1 watermelon
  2. Take 1 bowl basmati rice
  3. Take 1/4th chopped cauliflower
  4. Get 1/4 chopped carrot
  5. Prepare 1 chopped onions
  6. Make ready 5 spoon refined oil
  7. Take 1/2 cup green peas
  8. Make ready 1 spoon sugar
  9. Prepare 2 chopped green chillies
  10. Prepare Salt as per taste
  11. Make ready Handful dry fruits
Steps to make Watermelon Friedrice:
  1. Take one full watermelon. Now cut it on top making two parts and scoop out the middle portion. Then prepare the juice from scoop out watermelon.
  2. Now cook the basmati rice. Cooked it till half boil and drain the water. Then boil the green peas separately and keep it ready.
  3. In the meantime in a pan add oil. Let it heat. Then add chopped chillies and onions. Now add chopped veggies. Add salt & sugar and fry it for few minutes.
  4. Cover the pan and allow to cook the veggies for few more minutes. Once it cooked then add half boil rice. Then add watermelon juice.
  5. Now add dry fruits and green peas and mix well. Now cooked the rice fully.
  6. Check the salt, if required add more. Once cooked served hot.
