The ingredients needed to make Ayam kecap saus tiram👍🍅:
  1. Get 400 grm ayam potong2 cuci bersih
  2. Make ready 1 buah tomat
  3. Make ready 1/2 bawang bombay..3siung bawang putih (dicincang)
  4. Make ready Gula garam lada bbuk sckupnya
  5. Make ready 1 sdt saus tomat/saus sambal
  6. Make ready 1/4 sdt minyak wijen
  7. Get 1/2 sdt saus tiram
  8. Get 3 sdm kcap manis
  9. Make ready Air sckupnya
